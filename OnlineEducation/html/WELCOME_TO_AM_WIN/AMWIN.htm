AM-WIN is a fully integrated, WINDOWS, double entry accounting / book keeping system which has been written specifically for the Australian and New Zealand GST legislation and for the Automotive, Marine, Mower, Plumber, Appliance and other similar service industry businesses. AM-WIN also contains a module specifically designed for the Plumbing and Electrical trades, the Diesel pump and Injector trades and the Tool Repair industry. Generally AM-WIN is flexible enough to suit the majority of Service and Repair businesses. AM-WIN is produced and authored by AM-Win Software Pty Ltd and the current AM-WIN software is the culmination of over 32 years of development and expertise in the market place.

 

Countless hours of research and development has gone into ensuring that the AM-WIN product is compatible with both the CASH and ACCRUAL methods of GST. Not only is it capable of both, but it does both simultaneously and automatically so that if you ever change from one method of GST to the other, then AM-WIN knows what your change over GST amount will be. AM-WIN doesn't just work with the GST; it makes the GST a simple and easy prospect for everyone using it.
