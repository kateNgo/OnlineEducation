Invoice Print Options

 

INVOICE PRINT OPTIONS

 

A Print Invoice window appears. If you wish to print the invoice select Yes.

There are several invoice print options:- Many of these options can be defaulted by setting them in the Control System section.

 

Show Private Notes - This option if ticked prints detail lines that have a full stop (.) as the first character of the detail line. These lines are normally not printed on the invoice and hidden from the customer.

 

Tag GST Lines - This option if ticked places a * tag next to each line of chargeable items that attract a GST component in the price and prints a legend which advises the customer that the * means the items attract GST.

 

Print Header Details - This option if ticked prints your company details and letterhead at the top of the invoice. This should be unticked if you are using pre-printed letterheads.

 

Print Text Lines Only - This option if ticked will only print the text or detail lines in the body of the invoice. The parts, labour, revenue and other invoice charges will not be printed. The total, discount and gst amounts are also printed at the bottom of the invoice.

 

DonPrint Line Amounts - This option if ticked means that the total price for each line of an invoice is left off and only the invoice total is printed at the bottom of the invoice along with discount and gst totals. The qty and description is still printed.

 

DonPrint Labour Hours - This option if ticked means that the number of hours of labour on an invoice is not printed and only the description and total labour figures are printed.

 

DonCombine Labour - This option if ticked means that different labour charges on an invoice are all printed separately. If this option is unticked then all labour accounts are combined into one total labour figure and printed at the bottom of the invoice lines.

 

DonPrint Stock Codes - This option if ticked means that stock codes (part numbers) will not be printed on the invoice.

 

Detail GL Codes - This option if ticked means that the GL Code for Revenue and Labour items will be printed in the part number section on the invoice for each revenue and labour item.

 

Number of Copies - This option allows you to select the number of copies of an invoice you want printed. NOTE: This option is printer dependent. It will work on almost all laser printers, some bubble jets and almost no dot matrix printers. This is why after printing the invoice you will get the change to reprint it again.

View Hidden Components - In job invoices you have the option to hide certain detail or items from customers. However when you go to print the invoice, the print options window allows you to view these hidden contents by clicking into View Hidden Components field box.

If you chose to print the proposed Payment methods on the job card or Invoice the software will print a series of tick boxes labeled CASH, BANKCARD, VISA, MASTERCARD, CHEQUE, EFTPOS, OTHER. So that the customer can indicate on the job card what payment method they propose to use for paying for the job. On the invoice, if the payment methods are printed it allows users who print the invoice prior to the customer arriving, to keep a copy of the invoice with the payment details accurately recorded on the users copy of the invoice including cheque number details etc. This option can be set as a default from the Others > Control System area under the Options 1 Tab and is called Print Job Card Payment Details.

 

Printer Selection- This option allows you to select a printer the invoice will be printed to. The printer defaults to the invoice printer setup from the control system which is stored in the AM-WIN.INI file. You can then use the printer selection button to chose an alternative printer. This may be a printer in another room o ver a network or even a FAX printer so that invoices can be directly faxed to your customer through a fax modem.

 

After left clicking on the GO Button, a report preview window appears unless this option has been disabled through the control system. If an invoice is required, print the invoice by left clicking in the printer icon.

 

Note : that Tax invoice is printed in the top left hand corner to indicate that the invoice is a final tax invoice to be sent to the customer.

 

Offset from top / offset from bottom

 

Many users of the software have letterhead paper that they wish to use for printing invoices and statements etc. Because not all printers start at the same place on the page such as lasers and bubble jets which start at different places, we have included the option to start the page printing in a lower position and also to finish the printing at a higher position. To set the page position, under the Others menu within AM-Win and then under the control system option and then under the Business Tab you are able to change the Offset from top and Offset from bottom settings. 100 units is equal to an offset of approximately 2.54 mm or 1/10th of an INCH in the imperial scale. This option will only work for the invoice, credit returns, statements printer setting.
