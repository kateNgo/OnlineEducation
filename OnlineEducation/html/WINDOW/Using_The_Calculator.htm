USING THE CALCULATOR

 

You are able to access a calculator on your screen at anytime within AM-WIN.

 

Hold the ALT (next to space bar) and F7 keys down on the keyboard at the same time to access the calculator no matter which window you are in, in AM-WIN.

 

You can either click on the numbers on the screen using the mouse, or you can use the numbers on your keyboard. Make sure your Num Lock is on if you are going to be using the numbers on your keyboard.

 

Simply click on the X on the corner of the calculator to close the calculator.

 

You may also click on the dash _ at the top of the calculator (next to the X) in order to minimise it. This way it will appear at the bottom of your screen, and you may click on it at any time to make it appear again.

 

Within Job Invoicing there is a Calculator button which you can left click on in order to access the calculator instead of using ALT-F7

 

Please note, if you click out of the calculator, it will automatically minimise.
