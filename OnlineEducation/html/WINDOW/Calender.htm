Calender

 

USING THE CALENDER

 

You may access a calender anytime you are in AM-WIN. This calender is also accessed by clicking the button next to any Date field.

 

To access the calender from within AM-WIN, hold down the ALT button and F8 button at the same time. OR: if you are accessing the calender in order to choose a date for a date field, click on the button next to the date field. The calender will pop up.

 

By clicking on the single arrow buttons you may move forward or backwards by months.

 

By clicking on the double arrow buttons you may move forward or backwards by years.

 

If you want to select a date to be inserted into a field, simply click on that date on the calender. The calender will close down and insert that date into the date field.

 

To choose Todaydate, click on the Today button.

 

To close the calendar either click on the X, click the cancel button, or click on any date on the calendar.

 

Please note: although you can access the calender from anywhere in AM-WIN you can only use it to select a date for a date field if the date field has a button next to it.

 

Date fields which have arrows next to them, cannot be changed using the calender. The arrows must be used.

 

Please note if you click out of the Calender the calender will automatically be minimised.
