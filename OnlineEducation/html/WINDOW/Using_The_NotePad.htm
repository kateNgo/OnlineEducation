NotePad
USING THE NOTEPAD

 

You are able to access a windows notepad anytime you are in AM-WIN by holding down the ALT and F9 key on the keyboards.

 

Simply click into the notepad and start typing.

 

You can increase or decrease the size of the notepad by placing the mouse cursor at the corner of the notepad, holding down the left mouse button, and dragging it to where you want it to be.

 

You minimise, maximise or close the notepad by using the icons at the top right hand corner of the notepad.

 

Please note, if you click out of the notepad, onto another window, the notepad will automatically minimise.
