Discount Matrix

 

DISCOUNT MATRIX

 

The Discount Matrix is used to set a % discount to customers depending on what stock item they bought, what customer type they are, and the quantity of the stock they buy. Customer Types are set in Customer Masterfiles > customer accounts > in the Discount Matrix field, left click on the arrow and choose from a letter ranging from A  J. This sets that customers customer type to be used for the Discount Matrix.

 

Go to the Others menu and choose Discount Matrix. The Customer/Stock Discount Matrix will appear.

 

To Insert a group, left click on the button to the right of the Insert Group field. The Browse the GROUP File window will appear.

 

Find and select the group you want.

 

Left click on the Insert button.

 

Left click in the first range field and set the quantity of stock which must be bought to receive the first discount. The start of Range 2 and 3 are automatically inserted once you enter in Range 1- however these can be changed by typing into the fields.

 

Type in the size of the discount in the Discount column fields.

 

Once you have filled in the matrix for the group you selected, left click the Save button for the settings to be saved.

 

When a group is highlighted, the Delete button will delete that group.

 

Left click the Cancel button to close the Discount Matrix window.
