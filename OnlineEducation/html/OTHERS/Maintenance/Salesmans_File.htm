Salesmans File

 

SALESMANFILE

 

This option is used to list any sales people you may have, who are on a commission.

 

Select SalesmanFile from the Maintenance menu. The Browse SalesmanFile window will open.

 

Left click the Insert button to insert a Sales person. The Adding a Salesman Record window will appear. Fill in the Salesmaninitials, the Salesmanname and the territory the saleman works in- for eg: NSW, Sydney, East, South, Perth etc

 

Left click the OK button. And the information will be added to the Salesmanfile.

 

To Change a Salemandetails, highlight the saleperson on the list in the Browse SalemanFile window and left click the Change button. A Changing a Salesman Record will appear. Make the changes you want and hit the OK button.

 

To Delete a salemanfile, highlight the saleperson in the list and left click the Delete button.

 

Click on the Close button to close the SalesmanFile window.
