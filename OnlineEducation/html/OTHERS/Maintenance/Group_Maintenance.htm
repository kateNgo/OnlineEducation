Group Maintenance

 

GROUP

 

Use Group Maintenance to insert, view, change and delete customer/stock/supplier/job groups.

 

You may search for the GROUP you want by entering the Gcode.

 

To insert a group, left click the insert button. The Adding a Group Record window will appear.

 

Left click on the down arrow next to the Type field, and choose from Customer, Stock, Job or Supplier- depending on the type of group you are inserting.

 

Type in a code in the Gcode field.

 

Type in a Description in the Description field.

 

Left click the OK button to save the new group record.

 

If you chose to insert a Stock group, Purchase Discount fields will be added in.

 

The new group will be inserted into the Group window. In the Type column a letter will appear depending on what type of group you inserted.

C  customer

S  stock

J  job

P  Supplier

B  Sub Group

M  Mail Group

T - Teritory

 

To change a group, highlight the group you wish to change, and left click the change button.

 

Make the changes you wish, and then left click the OK button.

 

To delete a group, highlight the group you wish to delete, and left click the delete button.
