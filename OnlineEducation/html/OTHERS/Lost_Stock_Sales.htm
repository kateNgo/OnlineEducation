Lost Stock Sales

 

Recording Lost Sales

 

A new lost sales facility has been added which will allow you to record lost sales due to not having available stock on hand.

 

In the Stock Masterfiles under the Stock menu a new lost sales button has been added.

 

Highlight the part which you have lost the sale on and then click on the Lost Sale button (see below).

 

A lost sales window for that part is brought up.

 

In the customer field, type in the customer with whom you lost the sale.

 

In the qty field type the quantity of the sale that was lost.

 

In the notes section, type in any information regarding why this sale was lost etc

 

Click on the OK button when you have finished.

 

To View the information on all lost sales, go to the Others menu and choose Lost Sales.

 

The Browse the Lost sales window appears.

 

You may view the list of items either in order of their code, or in order of the date the item was entered into the lost sale log, by clicking on either the By code or By Entry Date tabs.

 

Within the By Code tab, you may locate a particular item by clicking anywhere on the list, and then typing in the code you wish to locate. The system will find the item and highlight it.

 

You are not able to change any of the details of these lost sales items in the log.

 

You can print a report of all lost sales within a range of dates by clicking on the Print button.

 

By using the arrows next to the From and To field, you may change the date range for the report to be printed within.

 

Once the date range is chosen, click on the Go button to see a preview of the report.
