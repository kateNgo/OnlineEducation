SALES ENQUIRIES

 

This option is used to look up sales information for stock items, such as how much of a particular stock item a customer has purchased, or how many sales has a particular stock item had.

 

Left click on the Sales Enquiries icon (13th icon from the left) or go to the Stock menu and choose Sales Enquiries).

The Stock Enquiries window will appear.

 

The Date Range for Selection indicates the date range you wish to search under and automatically defaults to one year from the date you are using AM-WIN. You may left click into these date fields and change them to the date range you want.

 

To find sale details for a particular stock item left click the button next to the Stock Item field. The Select Stock or Alternate Stock window will appear. Use this to search for and then select the stock whose sales you wish to enquire about. (See the Stock Masterfiles section on how to use the Select Stock or Alternate Stock window).

 

Once a stock item has been selected, left click the Refresh button. This will bring up all the sales for that stock item.

 

If you want to view sales information on a specific customer who has bought that stock item, left click the button next to the Customer field. A Customers list will appear. Select the customer you are enquiring about, and then left click the Refresh button.

 

This will then list the details of the sales for the stock item you searched for in step 2, with the Customer you searched for in step 4. Both the Stock Item code and Customer Code will appear in the Stock Enquiries window.

 

If the customer has more than one vehicle, you may left click the button to the right of the Job Item field. The Browse the Vehicle File window will appear. Select the vehicle you want from this window. Once you click the Refresh button, the Stock Enquiries window will display the sales information for the stock item you searched for earlier, for the particular vehicle you selected.

 

You may also enquire about a stock item and a vehicle, without searching for a customer. Hence, you repeat steps 2 and 6.

 

In order to see all the stock items sold to a particular customer, simply repeat step 4 without first selecting any stock item.

 

In order to see all the stock items sold for a particular vehicle, repeat step 6, without first selecting any stock item.

 

In order to see all the sales for a specific GL code, left click the button next to the GL code field, and select the GL code you want.

 

Left click the refresh button to see the sales for that GL code.

 

You may narrow the GL code information down and search for sales for a specific customer for that GL code, or search for sales to a specific vehicle for that GL code.

 

Note: you must always press the Refresh button once you do an enquiry, so the new information can be shown. For example, if you are doing a combined enquiry for a customer and a certain stock item, you press the refresh button after selecting the stock you wanted, and then you press the refresh button again once you have selected the customer you want. This will combine the results.

 

Hence, you may do a sales enquiry for stock alone, customers alone, job item alone or GL code alone. You may do a sales enquiry for any combination of these, EXCEPT:

You cannot combine an enquiry of a GL code with a stock code.

 

To begin a completely new enquiry left click the Clear button.

 

The Stock Statistics at the bottom of the Stock Enquiries window displays, for the stock item you have highlighted or selected:

 

Stock on Hand

Pref Supplier:  preferred supplier for that item

MTD Sales:   month to date sales quantity for that item

MTD Value:   month to date sales value for that item

YTD Sales:   year to date sales quantity for that item

YTD Sales:   year to date sales value for that item

MTD Purchase:  month to date purchase quantity for that item

MTD Value:   month to date purchase value for that item

YTD Purchase:  year to date purchase quantity for that item

YTD Value:   year to date purchase value for that item.

 

The Document Type field will indicate whether the document was a POS invoice, Job invoice, Credit Note etc

 

The Total field will show the total amount of all the stock items that appear in the Stock Enquiry window once you have conducted an enquiry.

 

Under Customer Statistics the phone and fax number for the Customer which is highlighted will appear in the Phone and Fax fields.

 

The amount the customer owes you will appear in the Balance field.

 

The Terms field will show what terms the customer is on. Such as 30, 60, 90 days, or COD.

 

The Mtd Turnover shows the total sales to the customer for the current month.

 

The Ytd field shows the total sales to the customer for the year.

 

You are able to print reports from the sales enquiries screen for customers/GL accounts/ stock/ job items.

 

Sales enquiries is found under the Stock menu.

Once you have completed doing an enquiry, simply click on the Print button at the top of the screen.

 

Click on the Go button on the new window which appears. This will then automatically print the sales enquiries screen.

 

Left click the Close button when you have finished using the Stock Enquiries window.
