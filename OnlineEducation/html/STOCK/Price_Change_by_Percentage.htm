Price Change by Percentage

 

% PRICE CHANGES

 

% Price Changes is used to increase all the prices of stock in a certain group by the same percentage.

 

Go to the Stock menu at the top of the AM-WIN screen, and choose % Price Changes. A Price Increase window will appear.

 

If you want the % change to effect ALL groups, then leave the Product Group field blank.

 

To choose a specific group, left click on the button to the right of the Enter Product Group field. The Browse the GROUP File window will appear.

 

Search for and select the group whose prices you wish to change, and then left click the Select button. This will return you to the Price Change window where the group you have chosen will be displayed.

 

Using the up and down arrows next to the Enter Increase Percentage field, choose what percentage you want to increase or decrease the prices by. You may type in the percentage rather than using the arrows.

 

Left click on the arrow next to the Increase By field and choose from Cost Price or Selling Price.

 

Depending on whether you choose Cost Price or Selling Price. Select boxes will then appear.Left click in the small boxes to select them.

 

You can select multiple groups to be re-priced by selecting groups one after the other. If you accidentally select the wrong group you can deselect it by clicking on the deselect button.

 

Left click the Clear button to begin the process again without saving any changes you have made.

 

Left click the Process button to process the changes. A window will appear stating that the process has been completed. You may then choose to make more price changes in other groups, or close the window down.
