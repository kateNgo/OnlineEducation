Stock Take / Transfer

 

TRANSFER/STOCKTAKE

 

Transfer/Stocktake allows you to do a stock take, or to do a stock transfer.

 

Go to the Stock menu and choose Transfer/Stocktake from the pop down menu. The Stocktake/Transfer window will appear.

 

STOCKTAKE

 

This option allows you to enter the Actual quantity of the stock items you have in stock. The discrepancy is automatically allocated to the 9010 general ledger account. If you wish to allocate the discrepancy to another account then you should use the stock transfer option.

 

A black dot should appear in the circle next to Stocktake near Selection at the top of the window.

Left click the Insert button.

 

Left click on the button next to the Stock Code field.

 

The Select Stock or Alternate Stock window will appear.

 

Select the stock you want and click on the Select button.

 

This will return you to the main window where your stock item will be displayed as well as the on hand quantity and actual quantity of that item is displayed.

 

By using the arrows next to the Actual field, you can change the Actual quantity of this stock item.

 

Left click on the Update button. This will transfer the information into the main part of the window, and will also display the Out By amount (on Hand quantity minus Actual quantity).

 

Left click the Insert button to insert another stock item.

 

Click on the Process button once the stocktake has been completed.

 

TRANSFER

This allows you to transfer stock in or out of the system and allocate the discrepancy to a particular general ledger account. For example, you may wish to transfer stock used on your own vehicles out of stock, and allocate the expense to a vehicle maintenance account. Or you may wish to allocate stock items to warranty account or to a promotions account if stock is supplied or used without an invoice being raised for the sale.

 

To do a Stock Transfer, left click in the circle next to Transfer near Selection at the top of the main window. The Actual field will be changed into a Transfer field.

Click on the Insert button.

 

Click on the button next to the Stock Code field and select the stock item as done above in step 5 & 6.

 

Use the arrows next to the Transfer field to select the quantity to be transferred. If you transfer a positive amount it will reduce the stock by that amount, if you wish to increase the stock, then you should transfer a negative quantity.

 

Click on the Update button.

 

Click on the Insert button to insert more stock items.

 

Click on the Process button when you have completed your stock transfer.

 

A Ledger Code window will appear.

 

If the ledger code displayed is correct, click the Ok button. Otherwise, click on the button next to this field. This will bring up the General Ledger window.

 

Select the General Ledger account you want, and click on the Select button.

 

Click on the Ok button in the Ledger Code window.

Delete button

 

Use the Delete button in transfer/stocktake to delete a stock item from the transfer or stocktake after you have already pressed the Update button.

Left click on the item in the main part of the window so that it is highlighted.

 

Click on the Delete button.

 

Clear Button

 

Use the clear button to clear all the details in the transfer or stocktake to start again.
