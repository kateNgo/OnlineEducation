Manufactured Items

 

MANUFACTURED ITEMS

 

This option enables you to manufacture items and take them out of stock, and place the finished item back into stock. You also have the ability to print Job Cards.

 

Go to the Stock menu and select Manufactured Items. The manufactured item window appears.

 

To make a new manufactured item you left click the New button and the Select Stock or Alternate Stock window appears automatically.

 

Left click on the Insert button. The Insert Part Stock window will appear. (see

the Stock Masterfiles section on how this window should be filled in)

 

When the information has been filled in, and the Ok button pressed, the part will

be added into the Select Stock or Alternate Stock window.

 

Left click the Select button in the Select Stock or Alternate Stock window (the

new item should be highlighted in this window before the Select button is pressed).

 

This will return you to the manufactured items screen, where the part will be displayed.

 

The date may be changed by left clicking on the up and down arrows next to the Date field, or by typing into the field.

 

Type in the quantity of the item you wish to manufacture in the Qty to Manufacture field.

 

By using the Parts, Detail, Lab, Rev, Text, and Kit buttons, you may add information for this manufactured item (see Job Invoicing Section of Manual for more details).

 

By highlighting one of the lines of detail you can then click on the Change and Delete buttons to change or delete them.

 

Left click the Save button, followed by the Finalise button to save and process the manufactured item entry.

 

Once the item is saved (and before it is finalised) you may print a job card for it by left clicking the Job Card button. The Job Card print options window will appear.

 

Set the options you want and then left click the Go button. Note: Tick Print Work Authority Text if you want the following text on the job card: ÂI hereby authorise the above repairs and agree to pay the full amount on completionÂ.

 

Tick Print prices on job card for the prices of the stock etc to be printed on the job card.

 

A print preview of the job card will be displayed.

 

Left clicking the History button (on the Manufactured items main window) will bring up a Manufacturing History window for all items which have been finalised. Highlighting the Job No at the top of this window, or searching for it by using the locator button will display the information for this item on the side and bottom fields in the Manufacturing History window.

 

The Reversal button (in the Manufactured Items main window) is used to reverse the job that has been finalised This option is used if a job has been finalised by mistake or incorrect items have been used and you wish to re-process the job.

 

Left click the Reversal button. Left click the button next to the History Job No field (which was previously the Job No field). This will bring up the Sel Manufactured Items window with a list of manufactured items.

 

Highlight the item you want reversed, and left click the Select key. Once you confirm that you want this item to be reversed, it will reverse all the entries on this job.

 

To change or add any details to jobs that are booked into be manufactured before processing the job as completed- left click on the Revise button. A Select Manufactured Item window appears with a list of manufactured items for jobs which have not yet been finalised.

 

Highlight the job you want, or search for it using the locator button. Left click the Select button.

 

The details of this item will then be displayed in the main Manufactured Items window- where you may then change the details for this job by following steps 9 Â 13.

 

Left click the Clear button to clear the screen without saving details, and left click the Close button to close the Manufactured Items window.
