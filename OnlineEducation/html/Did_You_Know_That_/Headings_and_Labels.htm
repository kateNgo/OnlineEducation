Headings / Labels

 

Did you know that you can change the headings of many of the fields in the Job item masterfiles? This means you can change rego number to serial number or other field labels to different names. You can also change the headings of the prices in the stock masterfiles such as Retail, Trade, Wholesale and Distributor. To do this you need to use the Modify System Labels section under the others menu.
