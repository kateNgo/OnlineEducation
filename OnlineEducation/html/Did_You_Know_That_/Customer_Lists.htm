Customer / Supplier - Lists / Letters

 

Did you know that you can print a customer list or the full details of a single customer in AM-WIN? In the customer mastefiles there is a Report button that allows you to print a customer list with name, address and phone and contact details or all the details for a specific selected customer. You can also print Mailing Labels or letters to a single or group of customers or all customers or to customers with a current outstanding balance.

 

Did you know that you can also do the same reports for suppliers in the Suppliers Masterfiles.

 

WOW! Did you also know that the Entities module that comes with AM-WINplus allows you to Print lists, labels and letters and also created a word mail merge file for selected customers and suppliers. WOW! Did you also know that you can select customers or suppliers based on groups, postcode range, when they last traded with you, a range of dates when they traded with you, the highest turnover customers by amount or by number, by the type of car they have and by the price level they are on and by what colour underpants they wear (well maybe not until they change the privacy act on this one). You can then sort the customers or suppliers into order of code, name, group, postcode, state or turnover. Impressed? Then have a look at Entities and let AM-WIN help you market your business better.
