Sorting Information

 

Did you know that on many of the screen including Enquiries, Payment register, Purchase History, Stock enquiries and other similar screens that contain list boxes that you can click on the headings above the information and it will be sorted automatically in order of the heading you clicked on? If you click on the same heading again then it will sort the information in reverse order as well. WOW!

 

For example, if you do a ledger enquiry on the 1020 bank account and click the Ref (Reference) heading or the job heading then the information will be sorted in order of reference or job.

 

If you do a customer enquiry click the Rego heading or the job heading then the information will be sorted in order of job number or by rego number etc.

 

Not all headings work this way but the majority of the common ones do. Have a play with this and you might find it very useful in locating information more efficiently.
