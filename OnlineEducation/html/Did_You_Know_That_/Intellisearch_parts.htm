Intellisearch

 

Did you know that you could search for a part by part number, description, cross reference, supecession and alternative parts details all at once or in any combination and also search for it by just a portion of the information. For instance by typing in RAK the system will find BRAKE PAD and any other part with the letters RAK in it. Great feature donyou think? Look up the stock search section to find out how. You can also click on the P button next to the cancel button in the select stock window to show all the selling prices for the highlighted item.
