Customer Intellisearch

 

Intellisearch has been added in customer masterfiles.  By clicking on the Binocular Intellisearch button in the customer masterfile, you can enter any part of the customers Code, Name, Address, Suburb or contact name to find the required customer. You then tick or untick the appropriate boxes next to the details you wish to search for. You can search for any combination of the available fields. You can also search by Group, Mail Group or Territory or by email. You can then make the search case sensitive if required by ticking in the Case Sensitive box. Once all your options have been ticked, click on the Find Now button and the system will then search and locate all the customers that fit the selected criteria.

 

When the required customer is found you can double click on the customer to display the masterfile details. You may find the search a bit slower than usual due to the nature and power of the search process.

 

Once the correct customer is found you can then click on the Stop button to stop the search prior to it finishing. If you stop the process too soon then you can press the New Search button to restart the search again. The new search button will also reflect any changes that have been made in the search text and required fields for the search.
