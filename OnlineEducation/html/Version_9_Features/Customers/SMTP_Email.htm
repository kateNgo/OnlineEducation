SMTP Email

 

SMTP is a Simple Mail Transfer Protocol functionality in AMWIN that allows you to send emails with out the use of a default email client program such as Outlook. To set this up you need to go to Others> Control System>System and Company Details tab. Click on the Security/Options tab and tick the box Use SMTP not MAPI to turn on this function. When you go to email a document in the usual manner (ie by selecting the print option of email document tickbox), the following screen appears. Just fill in the recipient details and anything else you wish to add to the notes box and click OK. The email will be sent via SMTP.
