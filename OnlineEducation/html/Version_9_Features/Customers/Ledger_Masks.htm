Ledger Masks

 

If you cannot access the ledger you need for a particular transaction, go to Tools>General Ledger Masks. From here you will be able to update the range of ledgers that are available under each of the nine different ledger groups. To change or update a range in a ledger group, Click on the group, for eg. St. Journal option on the left hand side of the screen. Then add the ledger you wish to access on the right hand side of the screen using the little square button and then choosing the ledger you need. Insert the ledger account for the other side as well. Once you have entered in the ledgers you need, then Save and Close from the screen.

 

You should then be able to see the ledger you need for your transaction.
