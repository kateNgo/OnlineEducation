V 9 Enquiries

 

Enquiries  ALL Option

In the enquiries screen, there is now an extra selection option of ALL. When you click on this option, and then press the F5 Refresh button, you will get a list of every transaction ever made for this particular customer.

 

Enquiries  Right Click to delete invoice

In the enquiries screen you have the option to delete a transaction. Simply right click on the transaction in the enquiry screen you wish to delete and select delete. This will remove the transaction from the enquiry screen, return all stock, reverse all revenue items and remove the invoice from history. This is, in effect, a quick and easy credit for an unwanted or incorrect invoice.

 

Please note: There are however, limitations to this delete function. The invoice MUST BE unpaid and the invoice MUST BE in the current month otherwise the system will NOT let you delete it.
