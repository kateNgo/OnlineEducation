Disable prices in Customer History

 

There is now an option to Disable prices in customer history both from within AM-Win and also from the separate history module. This allows mechanics or other staff to view previous service or repair details without getting access to cost or selling price details. To do this go to Others>Control System>System & Company Details, then go to the Security Options tab. Make sure you tick the box Disable Prices in Customer hist.exe.
