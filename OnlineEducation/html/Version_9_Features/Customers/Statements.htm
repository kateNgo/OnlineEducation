Statements

 

Statements are now available from within the main AM-Win menu. Go to Reports>Statements, then choose your normal statement run options.

 

You can now select a different statement format than that of the invoice format. You can change this from the Others Menu>Control System>Print Options and then change the statement format to one of the 8 formats.

 

You now have the ability to print a statement and selecting the print head details option from a balance forward statement. This will either print your company details on the statement or not, depending on whether you tick or untick the option.
