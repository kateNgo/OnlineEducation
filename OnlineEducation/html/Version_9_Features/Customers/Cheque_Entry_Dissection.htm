Cheque Entry Dissection

 

NOTE: If you wish to dissect a cheque into several different ledger accounts then you may do so as follows.

From the Ledger menu, Cheque entry option, Left click on the box next to Dissection so that a dot appears in it (located on the left side of the screen). When this is done, a new section of the window will added to the bottom of the window.

Left click the Insert button or hit the Insert key on your keyboard, to choose a ledger account to allocate some of the total cheque amount to. The Insert Mode window will appear.

Left click on the button next to the Expenses Code field and select an account from the Select Expenses Ledger window which will appear. The Expense Desc. field will automatically be filled in when the Expenses Code is selected.

The GST Types field will be automatically field in once you have chosen the Expense Account according to the settings for that account. If you wish to change this type left click on the arrow next to the GST Types field and choose from either GST, No GST, Capital Acquisition, Motor Vehicle: Type in the amount you want to allocate to that ledger account in the Expense Amount field.

Left click on the OK button. This will add this entry into the lower section of the cheque entry window.

Note that the Left to Post field at the bottom illustrates the amount you have left to allocate. The total field displays the amount which you have already allocated. Once you have completed allocating all money, the Left to Post field should equal zero, and the Total field should equal the amount of the cheque.

Click on the Insert button to continue to allocate to more ledger accounts, and repeat the above steps. At any time you may change or delete an entry by highlighting it and clicking on the Change or Delete button.

Once you have completed allocating the cheque, left click the Process button at the top of the cheque entry screen.
