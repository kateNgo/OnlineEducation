Settlement Discounts

 

Settlement Discounts  You can enter the amount of the percentage of discount you wish to give to this particular customer in the discount % field. The discount amount is calculated and shown in the discount Amt field.

 

Settlement Amount  this is the discounted amount, which you will need to collect from your supplier.

 

Settlement Ledger  If there is a difference between the invoice amount and the discount amount this amount of difference gets posted to a settlement ledger. Set up a Settlement Discounts ledger in the ledger masterfiles in the 9000 series to keep a record of the discounts you give to your supplier.
