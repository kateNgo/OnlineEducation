GST Exempt option

Export Customer  In the customer masterfiles screen you have the option to tick a customer as an export customer. If a customer is flagged for export then GST will not be charged to this customer. When an invoice is processed for this customer, the no GST tick box will be ticked on the invoice and GST will not be charged for this particular invoice.

You may also wish to make an invoice GST exempt where the customer is usually not exempt GST. This may be because items are being sent overseas on behalf of this customer whereby even though the customer is not exempt GST, the invoice is exempt GST because the goods are being delivered overseas.

There are also specific situations where certain items are not GST whereas other items are for a certain customer. The primary example of this is for returned veterans where some items on the invoice have GST and others do not as a result of special considerations given to this group of citizens.
