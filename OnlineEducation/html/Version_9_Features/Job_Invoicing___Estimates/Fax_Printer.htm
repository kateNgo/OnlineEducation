Fax Printer

You have an option to use a fax printer from AMWIN. You will need to select this option in the Others>Control System>print Options menu. Click on the Fax Printer Button to choose your printer.

When you print a document, and you get the print options up as normal, so then you tick the box which says  use fax printer and your document will automatically select the fax printer rather than giving you a drop down list to choose from.
