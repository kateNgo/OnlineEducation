Cut and Paste Function

We have improved the cut and the paste function. Simply highlight and click on the Scissor button on the right hand side of the screen. As you click on the scissor button you will see a pair of scissors appear on the left hand side of the invoice screen to indicate which lines you wish to cut.

 

Then click on the line where you wish to insert the cut items, and click on the paste button. The items which had the scissor icon on it have all now been moved to the new location.
