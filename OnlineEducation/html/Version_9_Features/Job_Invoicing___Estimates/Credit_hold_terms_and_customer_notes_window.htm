Credit hold / terms and customer notes window

This now appears based on a control option to show the status of the customers account, the customer terms and any recorded customer notes. An invoice on credit hold cannot be finalised. To activate this feature, go to Others>Control system> Business Options, and under the Job/Invoice Tab, under Invoice Print Options tick the box which says Show Credit Warning Message in jobs & Customer orders. Then when you open up a new job or finalise a job, the notes screen will appear.
