ENQ Button

There is a new button from the workshop job invoice screen labelled ENQ button. If you click on this button it will show you a summary of all of the parts used on this vehicle since the beginning of the financial year (as a default value) as a total QTY for each part used. This feature allows you to quickly view at a glance how many tyres, brakes and other parts the vehicle has used between two dates.
