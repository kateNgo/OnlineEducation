Subtotal Feature

To calculate a sub-total for a number of records in a job invoice, left click at the end of the record for which a sub-total is to be calculated. Click on the Total  Alt S button or press Alt + S keys to insert the subtotal.

 

For example, to calculate a subtotal for the first two records in a job invoice, left click and highlight the second record, click on the Total  Alt S button and a subtotal amount is automatically inserted.

You are now able to change any sub-total amount and AM-WIN will automatically reprice the parts and revenue items (but not labour). The pricing for parts and revenue items making up the subtotal will be increased or decreased on a pro-rata based on the amount of the increase or decrease made to the subtotal.

 

You are also able to change the description of the Sub Total, so that the description doesnhave to read sub total. Simply click on the subtotal line and insert the description you wish to have showing on the invoice.

NOTE: If you place a :+ at the beginning of the records and the Subtotal at the End of the records on an invoice, then the information in between those two functions will be hidden so only the Subtotal and the Value are seen on the invoice.

 

To delete a subtotal amount, left click on the sub-total to be deleted and select Delete.

When a subtotal is used in an invoice and the invoice is printed, the Sub Total now appears on the right hand side of the invoice and not in the middle as it did previously.

NOTE: You cannot print a subtotal if you combine labour on the invoice because the total before the subtotal will appear to be incorrect because the labour in the subtotal is now shown as a total at the end of the invoice.
