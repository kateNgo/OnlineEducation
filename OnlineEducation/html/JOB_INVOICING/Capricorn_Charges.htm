Capricorn Charges

 

Capricorn is one of several buying groups that many of our customers are involved with. In this scenario users of AM-Win may also be suppliers to Capricorn members. If this is the case then the system is able to generate certain reports and export files which makes it easier for the Capricorn supplier to report sales to members back to the Capricorn head office. When a Capricorn member purchases parts from a Capricorn supplier it is actually Capricorn and not the customer who pays for the goods. Therefore, the goods are delivered to the customer but billed to Capricorn who then pays the supplier.

 

If you are a Capricorn supplier then the following information must be used to configure your system correctly.

 

First you must have a customer called Capricorn (lets say CAPR01 but could also be any appropriate code) to bill the parts to. When you insert or change the Capricorn customer from the Customer Masterfiles you must set the Status of the customer to Third Party. This Third Party status lets AM-Win know that the customer is a Third Party (Capricorn or other similar group) customer. The system now knows to treat this customer in a uniquely different way to other customers.

 

Now the Capricorn (or other third party) customer is done you can insert or change the customer that actually orders / receives the parts. This customer can be inserted or updated from the customer masterfiles screen.

 

Firstly you must change the billto customer for the customer to Capricorn (say CAPR01) or some other third party company. You are then required to put the customers capricorn number in the Third Party ID field. This number will then appear on the Capricorn statements and also in the ascii file that can be generated to upload directly to Capricorn. It will also be printed on the customerinvoice.

 

Billing Rates:

 

Another feature specific to the third party status is how it effects pricing of the invoice. In normal active circumstances the invoice is charged out at the BILLTO rate. This means that the billto customer determines the discount rate, labour rate and stock price rate etc. However, when the billto customer is a Third Party customer these rates are determined by the customer and not the billto. This is because the invoice is actually for the customer and financed by Capricorn. YES this means it would also be appropriate to setup a finance company as a Third Party customer as they are financing the invoice for the customer in a similar manner to what Capricorn does.

 

Statements Report:

 

At the end of the month you will most likely want to print out a statement for the Capricorn transactions. From the statements program external to AM-Win you have an option to print out a Third Party Billing Report which is under the statements menu. This will print a statement for Capricorn (or other third party customers) in a format that shows the customer Third Party ID and other details that Capricorn require.

 

Statements Ascii File:

 

At the end of the month you will most likely want to produce an ascii file for the Capricorn transactions. From the statements program external to AM-Win you have an option to print out a Third Party Billing File which is under the statements menu. This will produce a Ascii file for Capricorn (or other third party customers) in a format that shows the customer Third Party ID and other details that Capricorn require. You can then email of FTP this file to the Third Party customer in the format they require it in.
