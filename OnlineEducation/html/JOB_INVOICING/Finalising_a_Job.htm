Finalising a Job

 

Note : Once a job has been finalised, the invoice is saved to the history file

  and the job is removed from the jobs in progress. You may then access the job to view or re-print it from the History File which is a separate icon on the windows desktop.

 

When a job has been completed, choose Final from the Job Invoice window to print a final invoice for the customer.

 

The system now has a separate password option for finalising an invoice as well as just being able to access the invoice screen. From the Others > Control System > p_levels 1 screen you can now enter a password level used for finalising a invoice or credit return. This would usually be the same of higher than your usually password level for access the invoice window. You can now have staff update the invoice but only someone with ah higher access level can finalise it.
