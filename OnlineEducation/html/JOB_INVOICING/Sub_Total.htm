Sub-Total

Sub-totaling

To calculate a sub-total for a number of records in the job invoice, left click at the end of the record for which a sub-total is to be calculated. Choose Total  Alt S or press Alt + S keys to insert the subtotal.

 

For example, to calculate a subtotal for the first three records in the job invoice, left click and highlight the third record, select Total  Alt S and a subtotal amount is automatically inserted.

To delete a subtotal amount, left click on the sub-total to be deleted and select Delete.

 

When a subtotal is used in an invoice and the invoice is printed, the Sub Total now appears on the right hand side of the invoice and not in the middle as it did previously.

 

You cannot print a subtotal if you combine labour on the invoice because the total before the subtotal will appear to be incorrect because the labour in the subtotal is now shown as a total at the end of the invoice.

 

Ability to Change Sub Totals & Sub Total Description

 

  1. 1. You are now able to change any sub-total amount and AM-WIN will automatically reprice the parts and revenue items- but not labour.

 

  1. 2. The pricing for parts and revenue items making up the subtotal will be increased or decreased on a pro-rata based on the amount of the increase or decrease made to the subtotal.

 

  1. 3. You are also able to change the description of the Sub Total, so that the description doesnhave to read sub total.

 

  1. 4. In order to change the sub-total & description you need to double click on to the Sub total. The Recost & Detail Group Amount window will appear.

 

  1. 5. In the Reprice To field type in the new sub total amount. If there is any labour included in the subtotal, the amount of the labour will be in the Minimum Amount field.

  1. 6. To change the description, type a new description in the Using Total Description field. Then press OK.

 

  1. 7. The items which make up the sub total will then automatically reprice in relation to the new sub total, and the new description will appear.
