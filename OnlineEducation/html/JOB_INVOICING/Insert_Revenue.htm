Insert Revenue

 

Choose Rev  F5 or press the F5 key to insert Revenue items in the record.

The Select Revenue Ledger window appears. Revenue accounts usually range between 4500  4999, 6030  6999 and 8001 - 8999, This range can be set from the General Ledger Masks.

 

Left click on the applicable revenue account and this highlights the revenue account code to be inserted into the record. Choose Select.

 

The Insert Revenue Record window appears.

 

Discount Rate - Enter percentage allowed for the revenue item for this customer.

 

Qty  Enter the quantity of the part or number of hours worked.

 

Price  Enter the price of the revenue item. This will be automatically inserted if a selling price is included in the revenue item. This can be changed from the General Ledger Masterfiles.

 

Units  Enter the appropriate unit for the revenue item.

 

Extension  The total amount to be charged for the revenue item. (automatically calculated).

 

Less Discount  The amount to be discounted. (automatically inserted).

 

GST  The amount of GST that the revenue item attracts. (automatically calculated from the GST settings in the Control System).

 

Total  The total amount to be invoiced for the revenue item, less any discount.

 

Note: The total amount displayed includes the GST.

 

C  Left click on the button to view the cost of the revenue item per unit.

 

NOTE: The cost price of a revenue item can be overridden by clicking on the C button and then changing the cost price. This is then used to calculate statistics under the F7 Button and also for cost reporting.

 

Choose More or Enter, if another revenue record is to be added.

 

Choose OK or F12, if there are no more revenue records are to be added.
