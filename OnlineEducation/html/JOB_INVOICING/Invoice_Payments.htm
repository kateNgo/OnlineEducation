Invoice Payments

 

Payments window appears ONLY if the customer is a C.O.D. customer or in other words the Account Terms in the Customer Masterfiles is set to Zero. The window will not appear for account customers.

 

Method  Click on the button to the right of the method field and select the method of payment or choose from the AM-WIN menu by left clicking Others > Groups Maintenance > Methods. The methods file window appears.

 

Left click on the method of payment by customer, choose Select.

 

In the Payments window, enter the amount, reference and cheque number, if applicable.

 

Note:  Customers wishing to pay using more than one method may do so by entering a further method and relevant details. Up to 3 methods can be used for one invoice. The customer may pay some by cheque, some by Credit card and some by Cash etc. You Cannot pay more than is owed for the invoice in this section. To pay additional amounts you should use the Customer Payments Section

 

If the customer uses different methods to pay off different amounts of the invoice, then repeat the above steps again for the other 2 rows of fields.

 

Once these fields are completed, you have the option to type in the amount the customer gave you in the Tendered field.

 

The change field will then automatically show the change to be given to the customer.

 

When all the payment details have been entered, Select OK.

 

 Note:  Any outstanding amount owing by the customer is automatically added to the customeraccount.
