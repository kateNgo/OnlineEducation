Suppress Printing of information on Parts, Labour & Revenue

 

You can suppress printing of information on parts, labour and revenue items sold in job invoicing. This is done by using the + sign in the details function within the Job Invoicing window. All items below the + sign up to the subtotal will not get printed in the job invoice

Left click the Detail F2 button in the job invoicing window. The Insert Detail Record window appears. Type in detail in the Insert Detail Record window. Click enter.

 

Left click the Detail F2 button in the job invoicing window. The Insert Detail Record window appears

 

Type in: +. Then click enter

 

You can now input parts, labour and revenue items and then do a subtotal

 

The detail you entered before the + key will appear in the job invoice when printed, but the information inserted for parts, labour and revenue will not. The sub-total amount which is the sum of all the amounts for parts, labour and revenue for the job will also show in the job invoice.

 

Any items on the invoice after the sub-total will also be printed unless another + is used.
