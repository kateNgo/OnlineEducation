Ability to Change Totals in Job Invoicing

 

You are able to change the grand total of an invoice while still in Job Invoicing.

To change the total of an invoice, click onto the button next to the total at the bottom of the job invoicing screen. You are now able to type directly into the total field.

 

Once you tab out of the total field, all the prices in the invoice will automatically reprice themselves in proportion to the new total.

 

Please note that the labour content of the subtotal is not repriced. Also you cannot alter the subtotal price to be less than the value of labour. You can however change the labour content manually.
