2.12 Repricing Job after itSaved but not Finalised

 

Use the REPRICE button in the Job Invoicing Window

 

Assume that you have added a part into a job invoice called brakes. In the job invoice this part item is priced at $100. Then you go into the parts masterfiles and change the price of the part brakes to $130. It is possible to make a change to the price of the part in a Job Invoice that has been saved but not yet finalised.

 

Open the Job invoice window and choose the REVISE button

 

Left click the button for the Job No field in the job invoice window

 

The Select Job window appears. Select the job invoice you want to revise by highlighting the relevant job invoice and clicking the select button.

 

AM-WIN returns you to the job invoicing window. Click the REPRICE button in the job invoicing window

 

The job invoice will now reprice the brake to reflect the new price in the Parts Masterfile
