Insert Labour

 

Choose Lab- F4 or press the F4 key to insert labour in the record. The Select Labour Ledger window appears.

 

Left click on the applicable labour account and this highlights the labour account code to be inserted into the record. Choose Select.

 

The Insert Labour Record window appears. Labour accounts usually range between 4010 and 4499. This range can be set from the General Ledger Masks.

 

Discount Rate - Enter percentage allowed for labour discount for this customer. (This may have already been inserted automatically from the customer masterfile)

Qty  Enter the number of hours of work you are charging them for.

ie- if Qty = 4, this could mean they actually worked 5 hours but are being

charged as though they only worked 4.

 

Actual hours worked  Enter the actual number of hours worked which calculates the cost of labour and is used in the staff performance report.

 

Price  Enter the labour rate per hour. This will automatically be inserted from the labour accounts which can be changed or added in the General Ledger Masterfiles. but can be changed manually. If a labour rate is set for the selected customer then that rate will be used instead of the rate for the selected ledger code.

 

Units  Hours (automatically inserted).

 

Extension  The total amount to be charged for the labour. (automatically calculated).

 

Less Discount  The amount to be discounted. (automatically inserted) from the customer masterfiles settings but can be manually changed.

 

GST  The amount of GST that the labour attracts. (automatically calculated from the GST settings in the Control System).

 

Total  The total amount to be invoiced for the labour, less any discount.

 

Note: The total amount displayed includes the GST.

 

C  Left click on the button to view the cost of labour per hour.

 

Choose More or Press Enter, if another labour record is to be added.

 

Choose OK or the F12 key, if there are no more labour records to be added.
