Insert Details

 

Choose Detail F2 button on the bottom of the Job Invoicing window or press

F2 key to insert details in the record. The Insert Detail window appears.

Enter Details in record and press the Ok or F12 button.

 

NOTE: If you put an Astrix (*) as the first character in a line of detail, the font size will automatically go to 14 and the text will print larger on the invoice.

 

NOTE2: If you put a Full Stop as the first character on a line of detail then the whole line will be blank on the printed invoice. This means you can make a comment about the job or vehicle or customer that you want in the history but not something you want the customer to see. To see the details with a Full Stop you need to tick the Show Private Notes box when printing an invoice.

 

NOTE3: When you enter details in an invoice, if it is preceded with a - (the key to the left of the + key on the top of the keyboard) then these details will automatically be inserted in the next job invoice for the same Job Item of the customer. The - must be entered on the same line as the text for each line you want repeated. Not on the line above or below. It is only the lines which begin with a - that will be repeated. This is used as an automatic reminder of something when the Job Item is booked in next time such as Needs Brakes Next Service

 

NOTE4:  Details, Stock and other charges can be suppressed from printing on the invoice using the + and Subtotal options together. See Suppress Printing for more details.

 

To enter more than one line, select More or press Enter. This will insert the

detail into the invoice, but it will keep the Insert Detail Record window open, so that you may enter another line. If you keep typing to the end of a line the system will automatically word wrap to a new line.

 

To enter details that will not appear on the invoice, insert a full stop before entering details. This detail will appear in the system, within the job invoicing section. However it will not be printed on the invoice.

 

Choose Font to change the font appearance of the record details. The Choose Report Style window appears.

 

Select the font, font style, font size and font colour then select OK to confirm . If you choose a font greater than 16 then the system will reduce the font to 16 which is the highest allowed.

 

 Note: The changes to the fonts etc. does not appear in the job invoicing window but will appear on the invoice.
