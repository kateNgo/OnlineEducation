Standard Job Descriptions

 

Left click on the standard job descriptions and type part of the description to locate it or scroll down and highlight the desire description. Double left click or Left click the Select button or press the ENTER key to use the highlighted description.

 

If you wish to INSERT a new job description then you can do so by left clicking on the Insert button or pressing the Insert Key and then typing in the new description. You can also change an existing description by left clicking on the CHANGE button. A description can also be deleted by left clicking on the DELETE button or pressing the Delete Key.
