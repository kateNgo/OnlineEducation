ASM Button

Auto Service Manager

 

The ASM (Auto Service Manager) button accesses the ASM program, which is a third party program, the software links to. ASM contains the service details for over 500 makes and models of vehicle including the service book parts, labour and service requirements for each regular service for each vehicle. You can then select the vehicle and service from within ASM and by clicking the TO INVOICE Button from within ASM all the scheduled service details including the parts and labour will be loaded into your JOB invoice that the ASM button was selected from. This works similar to KITS but with all the service book data already loaded for you.
