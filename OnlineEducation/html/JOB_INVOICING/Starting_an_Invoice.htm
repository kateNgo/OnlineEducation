Starting an Invoice

 

To Book In a New Job or start an invoice.

 

In the AM-WIN window, click the Job Invoicing icon (1st icon from the left) or from the menu choose Customers, left click and choose Job Invoicing, left click.

 

The Job Invoicing window appears.

 

Left Click on the New F9 Button or press the F9 Key at the top of the window. If you have already started and saved a job then you can use the Revise  F10 button or press the F10 key to recall the job and continue to update that job or finalise it.

 

Note: Choose Clear, if the New Job / Revise button has been selected incorrectly.

 

Customer - Enter Customer by clicking the button on the right of the Customer field or by pressing the F10 Key. The Select Customer window appears and can be used to find, update or insert a new customer.

 

Enter Customer Code for an existing customer or select the customer by left clicking on the customer. This highlights the customer for which the job is being carried out. Choose Select or double click to select the customer. .

 

If the customer is not an existing customer, choose Insert from the Select Customer window. The Insert Customer window appears. Enter the Customer Details (see Customer Masterfiles chapter on Customer Details). Select OK.

 

This window will also allow you to enter the details of the job item for the new customer. See the Job Item Files for more details. You can press the CTRL and + keys together to jump between the Customer and Job Item areas of the insert screen.

 

You can also enter Next Service details and Job Item notes for the specific job item by pressing the CTRL & Tab keys together or clicking on either the Service Due or Item Notes tabs at the bottom of the screen.

 

Note: If there is no Job Item for which a job is to be carried out and if General Work  F9 is being carried out on a part for instance, then the customer code will also appear in the Job Item code field.

 

To change Customer details, choose Change from the Select Customer window. The Change Customer window appears. Enter any details of the Customer to be changed and Select OK.

 

Job Item Button  You may wish to start a job directly from the Job Item number instead of the customer. Enter the Job Item Code by clicking the button on the right of the Job Item field or pressing the F11 Key. Find the Job Item code by entering the whole code or part of it, or select the Job Item by left clicking on the Job Item. This highlights the item for which the job is being carried out. Choose Select or double click the left mouse button. The customer will automatically be put into the job screen

 

Note: If there is no Job Item for which a job is to be carried out and if General Work  F9 is being carried out on a part for instance, then the customer code will also appear in the Job Item code field.

Enter any details of the Customer Job Item or Future Service Log to be changed and Select OK.
