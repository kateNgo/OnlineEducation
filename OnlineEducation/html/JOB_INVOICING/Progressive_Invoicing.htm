PROGRESSIVE JOB INVOICING

 

AM-Win allows you to finalise only part of an invoice at a time if this is what you wish. This is known as Progressive Invoicing.

 

To access this feature you need to go to the Others menu>Control System>Options tab. On the bottom right hand side is a field called: Progressive Job Posting. Right click into this field so that a tick appears, and then press the SAVE button.

 

If you want a Customer to always have Progressive Job Invoicing, go into Customer Masterfiles, for that Customer and click onto the Customer Accounts tab. On the right hand side click into the field called Use Progressive Invoice.

 

Once this is clicked it means that each time you do an invoice for this customer, you are able to finalise it progressively. Therefore in the job invoicing screen, on the top left hand corner the below field will be ticked.

 

You can always untick this field for a particular invoice if you donwant to use progressive invoicing for the invoice.

 

Similarly, if there is a customer that you rarely use Progressive Invoicing for but you want to use it for one particular occasion, you donneed to set them up as a Progressive Invoice customer in Customer Masterfiles. Simply right click into the Progressive Invoicing field on the top left hand corner of the Job Invoicing screen. This will make only that particular invoice for that customer a progressive invoice.

 

Once the Progressive Invoicing is activated you can begin your job invoice. You will notice that each time you add a part, labour, revenue item onto this invoice, it will appear with a green tick next to it.

 

This green tick symbolises that this item is going to be finalised.

 

If there is an item that you do not wish to finalise at this point, right click onto that item so that it is highlighted. Then hold down the ALT key and the M key on your keyboard. The green tick will disappear off that item, and you will notice that the total on the bottom of the screen will now be the original total minus the cost of the item that doesnhave a tick next to it.

 

Then click on Save & Finalise. The invoice which will print out will only show the items which had a tick next to them.

 

To Finalise the Rest of the Invoice

 

When you are ready to finalise the next part of the invoice, open up Job Invoicing and select Revise.

 

Find the job number of the original invoice. The job invoicing screen will now appear displaying the original 3 items, however the 2 items we finalised above will have a red P next to them to indicating that they have already been posted.

 

Now you can click onto the item you did not finalise before, and hold down the ALT & M key so that a green tick appears next to it. You can now save & finalise this part.

 

When you go into Enquiries you will notice that since you finalised this invoice in 2 parts, there are 2 invoices representing each time you finalised it displaying only the items you finalised at that time.

 

Please note that when you do Progressive Invoicing, you can change the Bill To details from one invoice to the next. This is particularly helpful for cases where you might have to invoice part of the invoice to the customer, and the other part to an insurance company

 

When you view a progressive invoice from the history module, if you double click or select Invoice for a selected job then only the details for that invoice are displayed/printed. If you click on the Job Button then the details of all invoices for the job are shown as well as details of any items on the job that are not yet finalised.
