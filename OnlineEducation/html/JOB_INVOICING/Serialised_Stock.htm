Serialised Stock

 

A stock item can be market in the Stock Masterfiles as a Serialised Stock Item. If this is the case then a window will appear at the right side of the select stock window which displays a list of serial numbers available for sale. You must select a valid serial number to be invoice.

 

NOTE: You can only select one serial number per line of an invoice. To invoice more than one serial number you must charge them as separate lines on the invoice. You can NOT sell a serialised stock item if you have zero (0) stock on hand.

 

The selected serial number for each item is shown in the invoice header below the customer contact and phone number details when the part number is highlighted in the body of the invoice.

 

Serial stock must be purchased through the Purchase Order section and not through the Purchase/Credits section.
