Job Statistics

 

Job Statistics  F7

 

To view Job Invoice statistics choose Stats- F7 or press the F7 key. The Job Statistics window appears. This window shows the profit for Parts, Labour and Revenue. NOTE: Consignment stock is included in the stock figures. Select OK to close window.

 

The window also shows the gross profit for each section. To the right of the sales/profit figures is another stats screen showing the number of items on the selected invoice. This is good to ensure you have all the parts on the invoice you should have. Count the number of parts on the job card and make sure you have entered the same number in the computer. You can then click the print button to print a copy of the statistics.
