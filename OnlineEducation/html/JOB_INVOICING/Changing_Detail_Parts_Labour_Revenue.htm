Changing Detail / Parts / Labour / Revenue

 

If you wish to change any details on the Job Invoice highlight the record by left clicking on any part of the record, then Select Change or double click on the item you wish to change.

 

A Change window appears for the relevant record.

 

Make changes to the record then select OK or press ENTER to save the changes.

 

NOTE: The cost, profit and margin for each line of an invoice can now be viewed on the invoice detail lines. This is initially hidden from the screen so that customers looking at the screen do not see the cost figures. You can then scroll across to the right of the screen and see the cost, profit and margin for each invoice line.
