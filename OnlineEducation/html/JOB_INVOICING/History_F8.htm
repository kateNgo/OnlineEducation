History - F8

 

History  F8

To print a report of the history of a customer and/or vehicle service select History  F8 or press the F8 key. The Vehicle Detail Report Options window appears.

 

Enter the Report Details by selecting the type of report from the menu. By left clicking on the down arrow next to the Type field you can choose whether to print a report for a particular Vehicle only or for all the Customers vehicles.

 

Left click in the Detail boxes to mark the details to be shown in the report.

Use the arrows next to the From and Until fields to select the date range of the

History you want printed out.

Select Go.

 

A preview window appears. If a report is required, print the report by left clicking in the printer icon.
