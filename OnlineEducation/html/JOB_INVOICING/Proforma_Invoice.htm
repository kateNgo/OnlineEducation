Proforma Invoice

 

A proforma invoice is similar to a normal invoice but in this case the figures have not be posted to the accounts and the invoice can still be revised and changed. This may be used to give the service manager a copy of an invoice which can then be changed according to the service managers instructions. It can also be used to give a customer an invoice which you do not wish to finalise until after you have processed your month end.

 

To view and/or print a pro forma job invoice, choose Proforma. A Print Job invoice (number) window appears.

 

Left click the Details to be included or not to be included in the invoice.

Select Go.

 

A preview window appears.

 

Note : that a Proforma invoice is printed in the top left hand corner to indicate that the invoice is only a proforma and not final.

 

Proforma Able to be used as a Delivery Docket

 

  1. 1. Within job invoicing you can now use a Proforma invoice as a delivery docket, so that when you print the Proforma invoice, the words Delivery Docket will appear instead.

 

  1. 2. Once you have completed the invoice and left clicked onto the Proforma Button, the following window will appear

 

  1. 3. Left click into the box next to Is Delivery Docket. When this is done, the donprint line amounts will automatically be ticked as well.

 

  1. 4. When you click on GO the print preview of the Delivery Docket will print without the sub-total, rounding amount, the GST and the total in the bottom right hand corner.

 

 

If a report is required, print the report by left clicking in the printer icon.
