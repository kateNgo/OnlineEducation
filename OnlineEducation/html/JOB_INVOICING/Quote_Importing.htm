Quote Importing

 

Import a Quote for customer  Alt Q

To import a quote for the customer, select Quote-Alt Q or press the Alt Q keys. The Select Quote (for the particular customer) window appears.
Left click on the Quote to be viewed and choose Select. The quote details will be imported into the job invoice and will retain the prices as per the quote.
If you want to view quotes for ALL customers, left click on the All Customers tab. This is to enable you to give the same quote to more than one customer. This will bring up a list of quotes for all customers.

Quotes can be made through the Estimates/Quotes section

 

Unlike a Kit, the prices from the quote are not updated when imported into an invoice, but stay the same as the quoted price.
