Delete a Job

 

You must first revise a job before you can delete it. Once the job is open, you are able to delete the selected job. A job can only be deleted if you have a password level of 80 or higher if you are using passwords.

 

Left click the Delete button to delete the Job. You are then required to enter a reason for deleting the Job which is saved into the Deletion Log file.
