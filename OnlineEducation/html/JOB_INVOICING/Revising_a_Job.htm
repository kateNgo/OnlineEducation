Revising a Job

 

To revise a job that is booked in and in progress choose Revise from the Job Invoicing window.

 

There are 2 ways to find a job. You can locate a job by the customer or by the job number. If you have over 20 jobs booked in at once then the customer search option may be faster. Otherwise simply display the jobs by job number.

 

Find by customer.

 

You may first click on the customer code and select the customer which will then display a list of all jobs for that customer.

 

When revising an invoice, if you select the incorrect job number for a certain customer you can then reselect the job number for the same customer. This saves having to also reselect the customer or selecting a job without selecting the customer first.

 

Find by Job Number.

 

Left click on the button on the right of the job number field to select the job to be revised The Select Job window appears.

 

Left click to highlight the job to be revised and choose Select. The previously saved details of that job number will be automatically inserted into the job invoice.

 

Within Job Invoicing, when you revise a job the Select Job will appear displaying all the jobs in progress. By clicking on the title of Date / Job / Customer / Rego / Make / Model / Initial columns you can order these jobs in progress in order of these fields
