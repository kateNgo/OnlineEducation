Invoicing History Button

 

The New history Button H/View available on the Job invoice screen allows you to view and print the history of all work done to the selected job item. You can click on the customer Tab which will also show you all history for the customer regardless of the selected job item. By then double clicking or entering on a selected job, the invoice is then displayed to the screen and can then also be printed out to the printer. The Ctrl-H buttons will also activate the history view option.
