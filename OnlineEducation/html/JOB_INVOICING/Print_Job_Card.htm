Job Card Printing

 

To print a job card for the customer, left click the Job Card button in the Job

Invoicing window (situated next to the Proforma button). A Job card for the customer window appears.

 

Select whether to print work authority text which will print a message at the bottom of the job card for the customer to sign which authorises the job to be carried out.

 

Select whether or not to print prices on the job card.

 

Select to use BillTo Customer, by left clicking in the small boxes. This allows you to show either the Owner or Bill to customer. It defaults to Billto.

 

Select the number of copies required and the number of ruled lines required. Select Go. NOTE: The number of copies only works on Laser Printers and some (but not many) ink jet printers.

 

When printing a Job card from either Job or Workshop Invoicing there is a tick box option which will allow you to show on hand and allocated quantities for each part that is on the job card. If ticked then a separate line for each part is printed showing the on hand and allocated Quantities.

 

If you chose to print the proposed Payment methods on the job card or Invoice the software will print a series of tick boxes labeled CASH, BANKCARD, VISA, MASTERCARD, CHEQUE, EFTPOS, OTHER. So that the customer can indicate on the job card what payment method they propose to use for paying for the job. On the invoice, if the payment methods are printed it allows users who print the invoice prior to the customer arriving, to keep a copy of the invoice with the payment details accurately recorded on the users copy of the invoice including cheque number details etc. This option can be set as a default from the Others > Control System area under the Options 1 Tab and is called Print Job Card Payment Details.

 

Left click in the Print Memo Details box so that a tick appears. Then click the Go button to see a preview of the job card. You will notice that the Job Item Notepad will appear on the second page.

 

A report preview window appears unless you have disabled the preview window from the Control System settings.

 

When a Job card / repair order is printed, the meter reading of the previous time that job item had been in for a service or repair is printed on the job card. The company name of the BillTo customer is now printed on the Key Tags at the bottom of the Job Card. These details can be printed or turned off at the time of printing the Job Card.

 

If a report is required, print the report by left clicking in the printer icon.
