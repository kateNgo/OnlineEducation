Service Log Screen

 

The Invoice date and Meter Reading window appears. You may change the invoice date and type in a meter reading.

 

Within this window you can change the date which appears on the invoice by using the Calendar button next to the Invoice Date field. You may also type in a meter reading. Future Service Log entrymay also be added by left clicking on the Insert button. Left click Ok. You can also enter the future service log and update the Memo notes field from this window.
