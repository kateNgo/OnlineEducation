Batch Invoice report

 

Under the reports Tab there is an option to allow the printing of batch invoices. This feature allows for the printing of Tax invoices in the usual invoice format in Batches. You can print a batch of invoices by date range, by customer or by statements range for current or last months statements.

 

You may decide that you do not want to print an invoice after every sale transaction but wait and print the invoices at the end of the month when you print your statements. This may be because you prefer to supply invoices with your statements rather than giving them to your customers staff who may loose them and inevitably at the end of the month after sending out statements you are required to send copies of several statements to your customer.

 

Each customer can be tagged to have the attach invoice option enabled from the customer master files screen. The batch invoice option for statements will only print for customers where the attach invoice option is enabled. However, the by date and by customer choice in batch invoice printing will still function regardless of the attach invoice option.
