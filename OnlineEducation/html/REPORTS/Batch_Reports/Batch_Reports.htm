Batch Reports

 

Go to the Reports menu and choose Batch Reports. The following window will appear with a list of reports in the menu Report Name

 

To choose a set of reports to print at the end of each month, left click into the circle next to Monthly- above Report to Print.

 

On the right hand side, left click on a report you would print at the end of the month so that it is highlighted.

 

Click on the Add button. This will transfer this report onto the right side of the screen:

 

You can then repeat this process for other reports you want to add to your end of month reports set, by clicking on the report, and then clicking on the Add button.

 

If you want to print all the reports, you can simply click on the Add All button.

 

If you wish to take a report off your list, left click on it on the right side of the page so it is highlighted, and then click on Back. To remove all the reports from your selected list, click on Back All

End of Year Reports:

 

To set up a set of reports you want printed for when you do your End of Year, click on the circle next to Yearly in the top right hand corner.

Then repeat the same steps as you did for your End of Month.

 

Other Reports:

 

If you would like to set up another set of reports, you can do this by clicking on the circle next to Others on the top right hand corner, and then repeating the same steps for adding reports as for End of Month.
