Staff Performance

 

This report gives you information on the hours and amount for each labour person or account, within a specified date range.

 

The Progress window appears with the date range defaulted to one month ago from todaydate. To change this date, use the arrows next to the From and Until fields, or click on the buttons next to these fields to use the calender.

 

You can also select a range of staff to be printed by selecting the from and to ledger accounts allocated to each staff member.

 

Left click the Go button to view the report. The report will display the following information for each labour person/account:

 

Charged hours, Actual hours, Average Rate, Amount Rate, Cost, Nett, Margin.
