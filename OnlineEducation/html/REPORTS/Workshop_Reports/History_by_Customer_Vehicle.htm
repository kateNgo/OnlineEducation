History by Customer/Vehicle

 

History By

 

Customer:

 

This report will list all invoices (written to a particular customer) on file for all job items for all jobs invoiced to that particular customer.

 

The date fields show the dates between which any customer histories will be printed on the report.

 

The dates will automatically default to one month from todaydate. This may be changed by using the calender to select the dates in the From Date and Until Date fields.

 

To print a history for all customers, left click the Go button.

 

To print a history for all customers, in order of the vehicle REGO number, left click the arrow next to the Method field, and choose All by Job Item. Then left click the Go button.

 

To print a history for a specific customer, left click the arrow next to the Method field, and choose Single Customer.

 

A new customer field will now appear, under the Method field.

 

Left click on the button to the right of the Customer field, and select a customer from the Select Customer window which will come up.

 

Left click the Go button once this customer has been selected- it should now appear in the Customer field.

 

To print a history for a particular vehicle, left click the arrow next to the Method field, and choose Single Vehicle.

 

A new Vehicle field will appear with a button next to it. Left click on the button, and select a vehicle from the Select Vehicle window.

 

The vehicle number should now appear in the Vehicle field. Left click the Go button to preview the report.

 

Job Item:

 

This option will list all invoices, between the dates chosen, for job items.

 

Follow the same instructions as above for Histories by Customer.
