Service Due Letters/Labels/Reports

 

Service Due

 

Letters:

 

This option is used to send Service Due Letters to all your customers or to a specific customer, for Job Items due for service between particular dates. See the Job Item Files section in the manual, particularly step 17, on setting up service due dates and setting up the service due letters.

 

In the Service Due Letters Option window, the dates will be default to one month from todaydate. These dates may be changed by using the up and down arrows next to the From Date and Until Date fields or by clicking on the buttons next to these fields to use the calender.

 

Click on the down arrow next to the Method field and select ALL if you want service due letters for All customers, and then left click the Go button.

If you want a service due letter for a specific customer, click on the arrow next to the Method field, and choose Single Customer from the drop down menu.

 

A new customer field will now appear, under the Method field.

 

Left click on the button to the right of the Customer field, and select a customer from the Select Customer window which will come up.

 

Left click the Go button once this customer has been selected- it should now appear in the Customer field.

 

The Service Due Letters will have todaydate, the date their service is due, their address, their vehicle code, and the letter.

 

Note: Any changes to the content of the letter can be done within Letter Maintenance in the Others menu.

 

Labels:

 

This option is used to print out labels of addresses for customers receiving service due letters.

The Service Due Labels Options window is identical to the Service Due Letters Options window. Follow the same steps as above, in order to print out Service Due Labels.

 

Reports:

 

This option is used to view a report of vehicles due for service between two particular dates. The report contains the vehicle code, the service description, the make and model of the vehicle, the date their service is due, and the meter reading.

The Service Due Reports Option window is identical to the above Service Due Letters Options window. Follow the same instructions to print out the Service Due Report.
