Job Items By Customer

 

This option is used to list Job items for all customers or for a specific customer. The report details the job items make, model, colour, the last invoice number for that vehicle, the manufactured date, and the date the last job was done on that item.

 

In the Job Items by Customer Report Options window, click on the arrow next to the Method field, and choose All for a list of job items for all customers, or Single Customer for job items for only one customer.

 

If you choose Single Customer, a customer field will be inserted. Left click the button next to the customer field and select the customer from the Select Customer window.

 

Left click the Go button to preview the report.
