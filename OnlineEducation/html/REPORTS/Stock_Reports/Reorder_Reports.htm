Reorder Reports

 

Reorder Report:

 

This report will give you the re-order level for those stock items that have the re-order data keyed in the Stock Masterfiles.

 

When the Stock Reorder Report Options window appears, left click on the arrow next to the Report Type field and choose from Reorder, Job, or all.

 

Left click the arrow next to the Include field and chose from All, Stock, Non-Stock, consignment, revenue & Trade-in. Refer to the Stock Type Topic for a full description of these items.

Left click the arrow next to the Sort Order field and choose whether you want the report details to be sorted by Code or by Description.

 

To insert the GROUP you want included on the report, click on the button next to the Group field and select the group you want from the Select Stock Group window which will come up. The group will then be inserted into the Group field.

 

To insert the Sub GROUP you want included on the report, click on the button next to the Sub Group field and select the group you want from the Select Stock Sub Group window which will come up. The group will then be inserted into the Group field.

 

Click on the GO button.
