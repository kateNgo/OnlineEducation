Consignment Field Report

 

Under the Reports > Stock menu there is an option to print a Consignment Field Report which prints a separate page for each customer listing the consignment parts for that customer. You can also select a specific territory (which a certain salesperson looks after) or select all territories. The report also prints a disclaimer with a place for the signature of the authorised person who approves that the goods counted and supplied were true and accurate. The customer notes details also appears on the report and can be used to show the people who are authorized to sign for the goods plus any special delivery or other notes for that customer. Please make sure no comments in the customer notes exist that you do not want the customer to read.
