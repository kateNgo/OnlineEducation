Cash/Bank Activity

Cash/Bank Activity

 

This option enables you to print a list of all transaction amounts posted to the Bank or Cash on Hand accounts, within a specified date range.

 

When the Bank or Cash Report Option window appears, left click on the down arrow next to the Source Account field and choose from the list of ledger accounts which will pop down.

 

Click on the down arrow next to the Information Included field and choose either Customers, Suppliers or ALL.

 

To change the date range of the report, use the up and down arrows next to the From and Until fields, or click on the buttons next to these fields to use the calender to select dates.

Left click the Go button to preview the report.
