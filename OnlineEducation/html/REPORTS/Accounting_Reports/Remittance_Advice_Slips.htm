Remittance Advice Slips

 

Two new options have been added regarding Remittance Slips.

 

Reprinting Remittance Advice for the Current Month:

 

Go the Reports menu and choose Accounting and then choose Remittance from the menu which appears.

 

The Remittance options window appears.

 

Click on the circle next to either Customer or Supplier depending on who you want the remittance advice for.

 

Click on the button next to the customer field (which will be the supplier field if you select Supplier).

 

A customer (or supplier) list will pop up, from which you can choose the customer (or supplier) you want.

 

In the Cheque field, type in the same cheque number which you typed in the customer payment/supplier payment screen when you processed this payment.

 

You can check what the cheque number was within the Payment Register (located under the Enquiries menu), and searching for the payment.

 

If the cheque number and supplier or customer donmatch up with each other, the system will notify you of this and close the Remittance window.

 

Once the two fields are filled in, click on the Go button.

 

A report will be produced displaying the total amount paid, as well as details of each invoice which was paid, such as the invoice date, description, age, invoice total and payable total.
