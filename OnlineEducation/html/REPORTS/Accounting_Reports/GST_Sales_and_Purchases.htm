GST Sales and Purchases

 

GST Sales/Purchase

 

Depending on whether you use a cash or accrual method of GST, this report will show you your GST liability, including - GST sales, GST free sales, export sales, GST purchases, capital acquisitions purchases, GST free purchases, and motor vehicle purchases.

 

Please note that the GST calculated is not the liability calculated on the Business Activity Statement (BAS). As the BAS also includes group tax, fringe benefits tax, sales tax rebate and other items payable on the BAS which are not related to GST. This report will automatically load once it is selected from the Accounting reports menu.

 

 

GST sales report Including GST:

This is all sales that have been recorded through invoicing, except for invoices.

 

GST sales report Excluding GST:

This is all sales recorded as an export.

 

GST Purchase report Including GST:

This is all purchases which are a Type 1.

 

GST Purchase report Excluding GST:

This is all purchases which are Type 2 & 4

 

GST Capital Purchase Report Including GST:

This is all purchases which are type 3.

 

The total of all these reports depend on whether you are on a cash or accrual basis.
