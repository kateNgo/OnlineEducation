Activity Report

 

Customer:

 

This report prints out list of all transactions, including invoices, credits and payments that have occurred for the customer.

 

In the Report Customers field, left click on the down arrow next to include to select from the menu: All, Single Account, Account Range.

 

In the Year field choose the financial year you wish to report on.

 

For the From Period field, left click on the down arrow and choose the from month for the report to start from and in the Until Period select the month to print until or the month to end the report.

 

If you choose All you will have an activity report listing all of your customers.

 

If you choose Single Account, the Customer Activity Report Options a From Customer field will be inserted into the window.

 

Click on the button next to this field and select a customer from the Select Customer window which will appear.

Left click the OK button.

 

If you choose Account Range a From Customer and until customer field will appear.

 

Click on the buttons next to the From Customer and Until Customer fields to select customers from the Select Customer windows. This will show you a list of customers to select from. Select the customer your require.

 

Left click the OK button to have an activity report for the customers within the range you typed in.

 

Supplier:

 

This report prints out list of all transactions, including invoices, credits and payments that have occurred for the supplier.

 

The Supplier Activity Report is similar to the Customers activity report.

 

Follow the same process as for Customers, except using Supplier codes.

 

Ledger:

 

This report prints out list of all transactions, including invoices, credits and payments that have occurred for that ledger account.

 

The Ledger Activity Report is similar to the Supplier and Customers activity reports.

 

There is an additional field under the Ledger report that allows you to tick the box indicating that you want to show the external account number details. This is the account number that your accountant uses which you can set up from the Ledger Masterfiles screen to assist your accountant in understanding your financial reports.

 

Follow the same process as for Suppliers and Customers, except using General Ledger codes.
