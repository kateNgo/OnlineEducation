Aged Analysis

 

Customer:

 

This report allows you to print out a list of current outstanding balances for all customers including the Total, Current, 30 day, 60 day, 90 day, 120 day and unallocated balances as well as the contact person and phone number.

 

There is also an option to print all customers, even if there are no outstanding balances (Print zero balances), and a further option to limit the aged analysis list to only customers with a balance of 30 days or more (Ignore less than 30 days). You can also print an analyses only showing accounts with a balance greater than zero which ignores negative balances and also print the report as it was for the previous month.

 

These options can be selected by left clicking in the boxes in the Customer aged Analysis window.

 

Supplier:

 

This report allows you to print out a list of current outstanding balances for all suppliers including the Total, Current, 30 day, 60 day, 90 day, 120 day and unallocated balances as well as the contact person.

 

There is also an option to print all suppliers, even if there are no outstanding balances. A further option is available for you to limit the aged analysis list to only suppliers with a balance of 30 days or more and also to print the report as it was for the previous month.

 

These options can be selected by left clicking in the boxes in the Supplier Age Analysis window.
