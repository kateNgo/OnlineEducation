Customer Report

 

The following report gives information on All customers, a Single customer, a Group of Customer, or Customers who have a balance owing.

 

Select Customers from the Customer/Supplier reports menu.

 

A customer report options window will open.

 

In the Report Type field, click the down arrow and choose from Detail Single, Group, All, or Balance Owing.

 

If you select Detail Single or Group a new field will appear where you have to click on the button next to it and select the customer or group you require.

 

Click the GO button when you have completed your selections.
