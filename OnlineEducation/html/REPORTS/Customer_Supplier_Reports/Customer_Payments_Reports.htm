Customer Payments Reports

 

Customer Payments

 

This report is used to print all customer payments or payments for a particular customer received within a specified date range.

 

In the Customer Payments Print Option window, use the up and down arrows next to the From and Until fields to select the date range, or click on the button next to these fields to use the calender.

 

To view payments for particular customers, click on the button next to the Customer field. A select customer window will appear from which you can select the Customer you want. Leave this field blank if you want to report on all customers.

 

Left click the Go button to preview the report.
