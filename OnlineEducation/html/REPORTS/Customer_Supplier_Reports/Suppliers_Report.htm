Suppliers Report

 

The following report gives information on all suppliers, a Single supplier, a group of Suppliers, or Suppliers wit a Balance owing.

 

Select suppliers from the customer/supplier reports menu

 

A Suppliers Report Options window will appear.

 

Click on the down arrow next to Report Type and select from Detail Single, Group, All or Balance Owing.

 

If you select Detail Single or Group a new field will appear where you have to click on the button next to these new fields and select the Supplier or group you require.

 

Click on the GO button when you have completed your selections.
