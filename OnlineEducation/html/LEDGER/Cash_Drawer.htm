CASH DRAWER

 

If you have a cash drawer / till set up on your terminal or computer then this option will allow you to open the cash drawer either from the Ledger menu option or by pressing the ALT-10 keys. There is a password level to access this option and a tick box that must be selected from the Others > Control Systems menu under the P_Levels_2 menu at the bottom right hand corner of the screen. The tick box must be ticked and the user must have an adequate security level for the cash drawer to open.

 

NOTE: If your cash drawer Is not plugged into a docket printer then ensure that you have selected the option of Separate Cash Drawer in the Others > Control System menu under the POS options.
