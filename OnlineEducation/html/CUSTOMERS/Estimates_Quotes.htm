Estimates / Quotes

 

ESTIMATES & QUOTES

 

This option is used for giving Estimated Quotes to customers or prospective customers.

Once the estimate quote is provided to a customer, the quote stays on file until it is deleted.

 

If the quoted customer decides to proceed with the job that was quoted, this quote can be processed into invoicing whilst in the job invoicing mode (See section on Job Invoicing and Quote - F4).

 

The same quote can be used for other similar types of Jobs as well.

 

TO CREATE AN ESTIMATE/QUOTE:

 

In the AM-WIN window, click the Estimate and Quotes icon (the 3rd icon from the right) or from the menu choose Customer, left click and choose Quote/Estimates, left click. The Estimate windows appears.

 

For a New Estimate left click the New button (or use F9). Then click on the Customer button to select your customer and Job item details.

 

In the Select Customer window, enter Customer Code for an existing customer or select the customer by left clicking on the customer. This highlights the customer for which the job is being carried out. Choose Select.

 

If the customer is not an existing customer, refer to the Job Invoicing chapter to Insert a customer or Change a customerdetails.

 

Refer to the Job Invoicing chapter to complete entry of the details for Detail / Parts / Labour & Revenue.

 

Once the details for the estimate is completed, select Save. A Quote no. (number) will be automatically inserted.

 

Choose Clear, to clear the Quotes window.

 

REVISING AN ESTIMATE/QUOTE:

 

Choose Revise, to bring up a previously saved quote for a customer and the Select Estimate window appears. Enter the quote number or left click on the quote to be viewed, then choose Select. The details for the quote will be automatically inserted into the Estimates window.

 

To delete any details on the quote, left click on the record to be deleted and choose Ctrl + Delete.

 

To change any details on the quote, double click on the record to be changed and amend the relevant information. Once you have made the changes click the Ok button.

 

Click on Save to save any changes you may have made.

 

Click on the Estimate Button to print the quote.
