FINDING A CUSTOMER

 

You can find a customer in a number of ways. If you know the customer code or close to it, then Left Click on the list of customers at the top of the Masterfiles window so that one of the customers is highlighted (usually in blue). Then simply type on the keyboard the Customer Code or part of it and the system will automatically scroll to and locate the customer.

 

You are also able to find the customer by name, phone, group, membership, suburb or alt code. You can do this by clicking on the appropriate tab at the top left of the masterfile screen and then typing the customer's name, phone, group, membership ID, suburb, or ALT code you are searching for.

 

If you are still not able to find the customer then you can click on the Magnifying Glass Find button at the top right of the masterfile screen, which will allow you to do an Intelli Search for the customer.

 

FIND BUTTON

 

Once you have clicked on the Magnifying Glass Find button and you have entered the Intelli Search screen, You can enter the search information in the search field which is found at the top left on the screen. For example, if you are searching for a mobile number, put the mobile number in the search field.

 

The next step is to then tick the relevant search function whether it be code, name, address1, address2, suburb, contact, email, mobile, phone, postcode, group, mail group or the territory. Don't forget to tick the case sensitive tick box if you require a case sensitive search. Click on the Find Now button and the system will do the search.

 

Once the search is complete, a list of the search results will appear. You can then select the customer that relates to your search and double click on the line item to view the customer details in the masterfile.

 

The stop button will stop a search in progress. The new search button will clear the results from the previous search. The exit button will close the Intelli Search screen.
