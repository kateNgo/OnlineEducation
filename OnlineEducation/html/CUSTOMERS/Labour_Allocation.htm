LABOUR ALLOCATION

 

Labour Allocation is used to allocate labour to different jobs, without having to go into each different job.

 

Go to the Customers menu and select Labour Allocation. The Allocations window will open.

 

To Insert an entry, left click the Insert button. The date will then appear in the Date field. You may use the button next to the date field, to change the date, if you wish to enter in labour for a date which is different to the actual date you are in AM-WIN.

 

Left click on the down arrow next to the Job Type to select whether the labour is workshop or manufacturing.

 

Left click on the button next to the Job No field. A Select Job window will come up. Find the Job you want, and left click on the Select button. This will then return you to the Allocations window where the job you selected will be displayed in the Job No field.

 

Left click on the Ledger button next to the Ledger field. A Select Revenue Ledger window will appear. Select the Ledger account you want and click on the Select button. You will then again return to the Allocations window where the ledger you selected will be displayed.

 

The Allocations window will now need to have the Started, Finished, Time Taken, actual hours used and quantity fields filled in.

 

Note: The quantity and actual hours used fields will default to 1.00, however these can be changed, and the other fields may now be filled in.

 

Rate: the amount they will be paid for each hour of work

 

Qty: the number of hours they are getting paid for.

 

Amount: the total amount they will be paid. This will be calculated automatically and cannot be changed.

 

Started: the time the work began. THIS IS IN 24 HOUR TIME

 

Finished: the time the work finished. THIS IS IN 24 HOUR TIME

 

Note: in 24 hour time, once you reach 12:00 Midday the time does not reset to be 1pm, 2pm, 3pm etc. It continues so that it becomes 13:00, 14:00, 15:00. Hence, if someone works 9am 5pm, you would enter 9.00 into the Started field and 17.00 into the finished field. AM-WIN will then automatically convert this back into normal time so that the window will read 9am  5pm.

Hence: it is entered in 24 hour time. AM-WIN will convert it back to 12 hour time.

 

Time Taken: this will be automatically calculated and is the time spent on the job.

 

Actual Hours Used: this is the actual time spent WORKING, and must be typed in. This should be the same as the Qty.

 

Left click the OK button and the information will be added to the blank screen at the top of the window

 

Then Left click the Post button. This will remove this entry from the Allocations list and move it to the Job Invoicing window for that Job Number. Hence, if you go to Job Invoicing and look up the job number, you will see the information you have just entered.

 

To Change the information, highlight the Allocation entry and left click the Change button. Change the details you want, and then click the OK button to save.
