BATCH INVOICING

 

Batch invoicing is a quicker and more simplified way of doing invoices for a customer. These invoices are not saved in the history files. The main reason for this section is to record manual docket book entries or service station sales for items such as drinks, sweets, cigarettes, fuel etc.

   

Go to the Customers menu and choose Batch Invoicing. The Batch Invoices window appears.

 

Find the customer you want to invoice by typing their code into the locator or by scrolling down the list with your arrow keys. When the customer is highlighted in the list on the left, their details will appear in the batch invoices window on the right.

Left click the Insert button. The Batch Invoices window will then go into Insert Mode, and the Date and Customer fields will be filled in with the current date and the customer code.

 

Enter the Invoice and Job item number into the Invoice # and Job item # fields by typing your invoice number into the invoice field and by clicking on the button above the job item number field to select the relevant registration number.

 

Left click on the Ledger button to select the GL account you need from the list. Double click to select your general ledger code. It will then appear in the ledger field below the button, along with the ledger code description.

 

Then Type in the amount you are invoicing in the Amount field.

 

All the fields should now be filled in.

 

Left click the Update button. This will add the information to the bottom of the Batch Invoices window.

 

To do another invoice for the same customer, left click the Insert button and repeat the above process. When this is done, the next invoice number after the one you just typed in, will be automatically inserted into the Invoice # field.

 

Note: Whenever you click the Insert button, for any customer, it will automatically give you the next invoice number.

 

When you have finished entering the invoices for a particular customer, left click the Process button to process all the invoices. This will remove the information from the bottom of the Batch Invoices window, and add the amount to the Balance amount. All other fields will be cleared.

 

You may choose another customer and repeat the above processes.

 

The Clear button will clear all fields without saving the information you have typed in.

 

The Delete button will delete the entry if it is highlighted at the bottom of the batch invoices window.
