A Day at a Glance

 

INVOICE AND PAYMENTS AT A GLANCE

 

Today at a Glance displays customers sales, customer payments received with the break up of methods of payment and the amounts and the supplier payments made from Cash and Bank 1 - 9 for a selected day or date range.

 

Note : Batch invoicing is not included in these figures.

 

TO VIEW A DAY AT A GLANCE:

 

In the main AM-WIN window, click the Invoice & Payments at a Glance icon (the 2nd icon from the right) or from the menu choose Customers, left click and choose Invoice & Payments at a Glance, left click.

 

The Invoice & Payments at a Glance window appears displaying the transaction totals for the current day. To change the date range, left click in the field where the date is displayed, and type in the date you wish to view or select it using the calendar button . Click on the Green OK tick button to refresh the screen.

 

The display window provides the details as to what the Total Invoices for the date range selected, any cost involved, as well as showing a gross profit for invoices processed for the date range.

 

The window displays the following information:

 

Total Stocks sold and the cost.

Total Labour sold and the cost.

Total Revenue sold and the cost.

Overall Profit and Loss

Customer Payment received, method of payments and the amount.

Outgoings made from Cash or Bank 1 to Bank 10.

 

Print  You can print the details on the screen by clicking on the print button.

 

Click on the Close button to exit.
