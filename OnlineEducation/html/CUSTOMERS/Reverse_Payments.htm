REVERSE PAYMENTS

 

The Reverse Payments section allows you to reverse payments without going into Open Item/Batch payments and doing a negative payment, and without having to issue a credit note when either of the following situations occur:

 

  • When a customercheque is dishonoured; and

  • When the payment you received has been credited to the wrong customer account.

 

To reverse a customer payment, go to the Customer Menu > Payments> Reverse Payments and left click to bring up the Customer Reverse Payment screen.

 

In this screen, click on the Customer button or press F10 to select the Customer. You can also search by Code, Name or Phone if you donknow the code. Just click on the search tab you wish to use and then enter the information into the locator field or alternatively, scroll down the customer list and left click on the customer and then press Select.

 

The window will then display details of the payments, which have been made by that customer. All of these payments will have green ticks next to them.

 

From the payment register details screen, left click onto the payment you want to reverse. The details of this payment will then be shown in the further description felds listed below and the details of this payment will also be shown automatically in the Cheque/Ref, Description, Method and Amount fields located above the listbox. You are able to change the Date, Cheque/Ref and Description details if it is required by left clicking on the bottom record so that it is highlighted in blue and then going up to the relevant fields located above the listbox to make your changes.

 

Click on the Process button to process the Reverse Payment.

 

Note: Next time you look up this screen for this customer, the payment you reversed will appear with a red R next to it.

 

You will then be asked whether the Payment has been banked? Yes/No.

 

If you chose Yes, then the entry will be reversed out of your Bank Reconciliation. You must chose which bank account it will be reversed from. Then click Process.

 

If you chose No, then it will take the amount from the customer account ledgers only.

 

You will then get a prompt saying Process Completed so just click on the OK Button.
