Summary Button

 

The Summary button takes you back to the sales order summary page where at a glance you can see details of what makes up the package deal, the accessories that come with the deal, any on costs there may be such as insurance for example, trade in details and any notes relevant to the sale. To see more details in relation to the package, accessories, on costs, trade in or notes just click on the relevant buttons to expand the information.

 

Once the Quotation is saved by clicking on the Accept button as the top of the screen, the document is automatically turned into a Sales Order.

 

By clicking on the Print button, you can then print the quotation. If the customer laster decides to all something additional, this can be done and another quotation supplied to the customer.

 

A jobcard can be created for the workshop for any additional work which is now required to be done on the Item from the Package button.

 

If you require further work to be done on the Traded Item, the jobcard can be produced by clicking on the Jobcard button in the Trade In button. The job will automatically be created to the internal customer which is yourself.
