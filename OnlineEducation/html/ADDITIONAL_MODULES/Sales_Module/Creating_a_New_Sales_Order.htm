Creating a New Sales Order

 

To create a new sales order or quotation, you need to click on the New  F9 button from the sales order screen which is under the Sales order option of the program menu or accessed from the fourth icon in the toolbar.

 

When a new sales order is selected, you must first select a prospect from the Prospect database and the document will start off with a heading of Quotation.

 

Prospect button  (also manually adding a new prospect) the Prospect button allows you to select or insert a new prospect into the quotation. When you click on the Prospect button you will be taken to the prospect screen where you can search for an existing prospect by the membership number, name, suburb, phone number or customer. A prospect differs from a customer in that the prospect may not ever become a customer as you may provide many quotations to prospects who never actually purchase an item from you. A prospect does not become a customer until the quotation is accepted and becomes a sales order.

 

You can then select a prospect by highlighting the required prospect and clicking on the select button. If the prospect is a new prospect that has not previously been a prospect or customer you can then click on the insert button at the top of the screen and insert a new prospect. This allows you to insert the prospectname, drivers licence number, date of birth, address, contact details and other information relating to the prospect.

 

You can also record a picture of the customer or a scan of their driverlicence and store this in the system depending on the privacy laws that govern your State. Once the details have been entered, you can then save the prospect and select them to proceed with the sales quotation.

 

Existing customer  a new prospect may actually be an existing customer where you have been servicing equipment for that customer but may have not previously sold an Item to them. If the prospect is an existing customer, then you can click on the existing customer button in the prospect database and the system will create a new prospect using existing data from your customer database. You can then add additional prospect information such as their driverlicence, date of birth, occupation, hobbies and other details you wish to record against the prospect.

 

Creating a customer  If the existing prospect is not already a customer, and you wish to create them as a customer, you can do so by clicking on the create customer button directly above the existing customer button, once the prospect details have been saved. This will create a customer in your customer database using the existing details of the prospect.

 

Once the prospect has been selected you can then proceed with the quotation/sales order.

 

The salesperson button can be used to select a salesperson from the salesperson database. A salesperson must be allocated to a quotation before the quotation is saved.

 

You are then able to select the person who will manage the sale using the managed by button. However, it is not necessary to select this option. You can also select or manually type a description of the quotation using the description button or simply by typing in a description. This description will make finding the quotation easier at a later date. You are also then able to enter a source such as Yellow Pages, by word of mouth or other referral sources for the sale.

 

You can then enter the valid for days and the location of the sales order, You can only enter a location which you as an operator have access to.

 

The system automatically generates a created date and time that the quotation was created.

 

At this stage, you most likely will not be entering an expected delivery date and time or an ordered date and order number. However, if you are creating a sales order where a sale has already been accepted then you may wish to add this data. You can add this information at any time during the sales process until the sale is finalised.

 

You can also enter a follow up date so that you can follow up the quotation at a future time.

 

The operator is automatically assigned as the operator who is currently logged into the system when the quotation was created.

 

You can then Save the quotation by clicking on the save button at the top of the screen. You must click on the save button PRIOR to being able to enter any further details of the quotation such as the package, accessories or other records.

 

You can also use the save/exit button to save the quotation. If you use the save/exit button then you will be returned to the previous sales order screen and will exit from the current sales order window.

 

Once the sales order has been saved it automatically generates a consecutive document number and you are then able to add additional details such as the package and any accessories, on costs, trade ins or notes to the quotation.
