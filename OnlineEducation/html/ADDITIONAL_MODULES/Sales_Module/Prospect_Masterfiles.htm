Prospect Masterfiles

 

Prospect Masterfile

 

Quotes Tab - this tab contains information on any previous sales orders they may have had, information on any follow up calls, letters or marketing techniques and also survey information if any have been conducted in relation to this prospect.

 

New Survey button  click on this button to create a survey. You can insert any relevant questions you may want to include, along with the rating details and any further explanations the prospect may have added. To save the information just click on OK. You can then use the Change Survey button to change details or to add new questions.

 

By clicking on the Question Set button you can insert a new set of Survey questions. Mutiple Survey sets may be added.

 

Insert order button F9  when you click on this button the system will take you to the Sales module where you can then add another sales order into the system using your prospectdetails.

 

Revise order button F10  when you click on this button the system will take you to the sales order screen where you can revise a previous order you may have saved for that that prospect.

 

Follow ups - this is where you can record details of any follow up that have been conducted in relation to that prospect. To add follow up details just click on the Insert button and a new screen will pop up. You can then go through and enter any notes and details about the follow up and any customer comments or information which may have been obtained as a result of the follow up calls. When all the details have been entered just click OK to save the information. Then if required, use the change and delete buttons to edit the comments.

 

Notes/Membership Tab - this is an area where you can record any relevant notes applicable to this particular prospect. The spellcheck button can be used to check the spelling and grammar of any text which is typed into the notes section.

 

If you wish to set up Membership Details where you customer may earn reward points which can be redeemed at a later date, this can be set up in this area. Customer details are shown and job item details can also be added to the screen.

 

Changing a prospectDetails - to change existing details in your prospect file search the listbox for your prospect and once the blue scroll bar has highlighted your record the details should show up in the second half of the screen. Then simply enter or change the new details in the relevant fields and click on the Save button.
