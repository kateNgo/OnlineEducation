Package Details

 

A package may consist of a single Item or piece of equipment such as a car, boat, caravan, tractor etc or may consist of any of these items plus additional items that form a package. The package may include for example, a boat, trailer and one or more motors which go towards providing a complete package. The package may also include various accessories and on costs such as radios, GPS units, insurance, dealer delivery charges etc.

 

To select a package you must first click on the Package Button either using the button on the left of the screen under the document number or on the right of the screen under the summary button.

 

You can then click on the Insert button, which will allow you to select an Item to be inserted into the package area. Once the Item is selected, all of the details of the Item as well as any attachments, accessories, on costs and other Item details are automatically included in the quotation. However, only accessories and on costs which have been tagged as fitted to the Item will be included in the quotation.

 

You may then modify certain details about the Item such as colour etc and also modify the selling price based on the minimum sale and managerprice.

 

You are not able to sell an item at below the minimum sale price and managerprice unless you are a system administrator (usually the business owner). You can then use the save button to save the package. A package can be deleted from the quotation using the delete button. However, it cannot be deleted or modified once the order has been either finalised or completed.

 

The history button allows you to view the history of any workshop jobs or pre-delivery jobs that have been done to the selected Item.

 

Once the package is saved, then the attachments, accessories and on costs associated with this package are also saved and the package total figures are shown on the right hand side of the screen. A picture of the Item and attachments can also be seen and up to four pictures of the Item or attachments can be viewed.

 

You can then use the other save button at the top of the screen or the save/exit button to save all of the details of the quotation/sales order.

 

To return to the summary screen you can then click on the Summary button under the picture at the top right of the screen and this will return you to the Summary tab where you can view all of the details of the quotation/order.

 

When changing from one tab to another using either the buttons on the left or right hand side, all of the details entered in the previous tab get saved.
