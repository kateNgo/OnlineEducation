Sales Module Payments Button

 

Payments Button

 

The payments button at the bottom right hand corner of the screen shows details of any payments made in relation to this Item. You can also process a payment from this screen by clicking on the Customer Payment Button.

 

Once you have clicked on the customer payment button a new screen opens. This is where you can process your customer payments. From this screen you can select the relevant date (by clicking on the date button), and selecting the method of payment by clicking on the Method button. You can then insert the description, cheque references and amounts and then click on the accept button. You can then allocate this payment to any unpaid invoices by double clicking on the invoice contained in the bottom listbox. Once you have tagged the invoice for payment, then you can click on the process button to complete the transaction.

 

This area is used to process deposit payments as well as all other payments associated with this sale. It also includes payments for finance company amounts or any other payment methods. You can also record payments of vouchers here.

 

The delivery details are also recorded here for ease of reference. This section also contains finance details, such as the finance company details, financed amounts, interest rates and payment terms and amount.
