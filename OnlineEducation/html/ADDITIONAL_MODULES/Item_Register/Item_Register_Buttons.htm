Item Register Buttons

 

Internal Items are Items that are owned by the business and used by the business for its own internal purposes. These Items would include company cars (but not if they are for sale or hire), computers used to run the business, fax, photocopier, office furniture and equipment, machines used in the production of goods, machines used to assist with performing a job etc.

 

For Hire Items are Items which are used to hire out to customers. These can be cars, boats, lawnmowers, forklifts, tools etc. or any equipment that is available for and used to hire out.

 

For Sale Items are Items that are available for sale such as cars, boats, lawnmowers etc. An Item must be made available for sale before it can be sold through the invoice module.

 

The All button will display all Items in the list regardless of the category they are in.
