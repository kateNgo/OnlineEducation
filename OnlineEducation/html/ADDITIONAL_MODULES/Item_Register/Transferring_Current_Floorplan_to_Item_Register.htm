Transferring Current Floorplan Items To Item Register

 

The first step is to enter all of your current floorplan items into the Item Register. This process is only required as part of the initial setup.

 

A few things which need to be set up first. Your ledgers are already setup, however, if you wish to split the sale and purchase of boats/motors/trailers, you could then used 6101 and 7101 for motors and 6102 and 7102 for trailers. You must remember then, that when you create the Item you will need to change the ledger number in the Ledger/Expenses tab from the default ledger which is already set.

 

Next you need to create a customer in the Customer Masterfile under your own business name. The status will need to be . In the Account Details tab you will need to set up the Offset Ledgers. The ledger in each field will be an expense account eg., 7150, and called, perhaps, Sales Workshop work. This ledger will get updated whenever a jobcard is created and finalised from the Sales Module.

 

Your floorplan suppliers eg., GE, Capital Finance and Yamaha will need to be ticked as floorplan suppliers in the Supplier Masterfile, in the Options/Deliveries/Notes tab..

 

In the Control System > Invoice Tab > Last Assignment Nos, change the number in the Item# field to 10,000 and Save.

 

You are now ready for the first stage of entering the Floorplan information.

 

Go to the Item Register. Click on Insert, and enter required data into the following fields. You will need to enter a catalogue item for each Make and Model of an item you have on floorplan, eg., if you have 6 boats which are 50hp Evinrude, you only need to enter the catalogue item once.

 

The Catalogue item is used as a template to access the information from purchase orders to bring the individual items into the Item Register under their own serial/chassis nos.

 

Item Category  Will automatically prompt to For Sale or for hire depending on your control file settings. .

 

Item Type - Select with the item is a Boat, Motorcycle, Vehicle etc. If these options are not yet setup, then click on Insert, type in Boat, and Save.

 

Status  Select Catalogue. The reason Catalogue is chosen, is that the item is not part of stock file at this stage.

 

Make/Model  Click on this button and select the correct Make and Model.

 

Owner  When you click on the Owner button, select the customer you have created as the internal customer in your own business name.

Now click on Save

 

The 2nd stage is to enter the floorplan items through the Purchase Order screen to receive the stock into the Item register and enable payment to be made when the item is sold. You need to create a purchase order for each floorplan item.

 

If you have any current floorplan items on job invoices, do not enter these floorplan items as a purchase order. Simply finalise and handle the entries for that floorplan as you would have in the past until the last of the original floorplan items on current jobs have been finalised.

 

In the Purchase Order screen, start a new order and choose the stock supplier eg., Mercury, Mitsubishi, Honda. Change the Bill To, to be your floorplan supplier eg., GE or Capital Finance. Note that the purchase type will change to Floorplan.

 

Click on the Catalogue button in the bottom left hand corner of the screen and select the Item or Attachment required.

 

Once the item is selected the Insert Item Record screen will appear. Additional information can be inserted into the screen at this point, if required.

Click on the OK button and this item will be added to the purchase order.

 

Click on Save and Receive. The Trust Receipt Details screen will then appear. Fill in the relevant details. Please note, that the Interest Free Days field should display the number of days left from the date of entering the information onto the purchase order. Click on OK.

 

A screen will then appear and you will be required to enter information in to at least the first field at the bottom of the screen eg., hull or chassis number.

 

Ignore the button at the bottom right hand corner. This will only be relevant when you are past the stage of entering your initial floorplan stock and are then selling through the Sales Module.

 

Click on the Save button, then the Completed button.

 

You will then be returned to the Purchase Order Screen. Click on the Save button The floorplan Purchase Order does not get finalised.

 

The details of the floorplan items will now show in the Item Register.

 

Payment of the floorplan items can then be made through the Sales Module.
