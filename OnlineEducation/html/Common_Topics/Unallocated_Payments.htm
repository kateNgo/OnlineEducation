Unallocated Payments

Ability to process payments through Cust. Payments without allocating to outstanding invoices

 

You are now able to process a payment from a Customer without allocating it to an outstanding invoice

 

Open Customer Payments, and fill out the required fields as usual. However once you press the Accept button, instead of tagging invoices, simply press the Process button straight afterwards.

 

The payment amount will be taken from the balance on the customers account, and will be displayed in the Enquiries screen under Unallocated Balance

 

 

Reallocating an unallocated amount

 

If you have processed a payment and not allocated it to an invoice, and then decide that you do want the amount to come off an invoice, you must firstly set up a new payment method called Alloc.

Go to the Others menu, to Maintenance and then choose Method

 

In the Method File window which appears, click on the Insert button. The Adding Method File record window appears.

 

In the Method field type ALLOC. In the Method Code field choose General Ledger account 1010. Choose a Type for this account as well. Then click onto the OK button to save it.

Go into your payments window and bring up the customer whose payment you want to reallocate.

 

In the Method field choose the ALLOC. Method. Type in a reference number and the amount which was previously unallocated that you want to allocate.

 

Press Accept and tag the invoice you want the amount to be allocated to, and then click on Process

 

Within Enquiries you will now notice that the Unallocated balance should be zero (if you reallocated the whole amount) and the amount in the terms field should have changed. The total balance should remain the same.
