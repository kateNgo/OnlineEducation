Select Stock or Alternate Stock Window

 

Enter a word or part of a word that is related to the Stock Item Code in the Locator field. Choose Search and this will highlight the Stock Item to be viewed in the Sales Enquires. Choose Select and the Stock Item Code will be automatically inserted in the Sales Enquiries window.

 

Click the Description tab. A search can be similarly conducted as in Step 45 but this time using a word or part of a word that is related to the description of the Stock Item.

 

A similar search can also be conducted using X-ref of a Stock Item by clicking on the X-Ref tab.

 

Alternatively, choose Search. The Intelli-Search on Stock window appears.

 

Enter a word or part of a word that is related to the Stock Item or Alternative Stock item in the Containing field. Tick the boxes by clicking the matches to be found in the Stock File or Alternative Stock File. Choose Find Now.

 

If the search is successful, the Stock item/s will appear. Left click on the stock item to be viewed in the sales enquiries. This highlights the stock item code, which will be automatically inserted. Choose Select.

 

Choose Stop, to abort a search.

 

Note: The search may need to be aborted due to the word or part of the word being too insufficient and needs to be further defined for the search to be conducted.

 

Choose New Search, to conduct a new search.

 

From this section you can also record lost sales for the selected stock item.

 

The [S] button opens up the stock masterfile screen for the selected stock item.

 

The [P] button displays the cost and all selling prices for the selected stock item.

 

To insert part stock, choose Insert from the Select Stock or Alternate Stock window. A Insert Part Stock window appears.

 

 

Code - Enter Stock Code.

 

Units  Enter number of units of Part Stock Item.

 

Description  Enter description of Part Stock Item.

 

Cross Reference  Enter Cross reference for Part Stock Item.

 

Attracts GST?  Click whether Part Stock Item attracts the GST.

 

Group - Enter the group which the Part Stock Item belongs to by clicking on the button on the right of the group field. The Select Stock GROUP window appears. Enter the group code or left click the applicable Group. This highlights the group to be automatically inserted in the group field, then choose Select.

 

Retail  Enter retail price for stock item.

 

Trade  Enter trading price for stock item.

 

W/Sale  Enter wholesale price for stock item.

 

N/Sale  Enter normal wholesale price for stock item.

 

Rec  Enter recommended retail price for stock item.

 

Ex GST  Enter price of the stock before the imposition of the GST.

 

Inc GST  Enter price of the stock including imposition of the GST.

 

GL Codes - Enter general ledger codes to create Asset, Revenue, Expense, Discrepancy accounts to individual stock items.
