Payment Register

 

PAYMENT- REGISTER

 

This option is used for: displaying payment made by a customer.

and displaying payments made to a supplier.

 

Viewing the Payment Register

 

In the AM-WIN window, choose Enquiry from the menu, left click and choose Payment-Register, left click.

 

The Payment Register window appears.

 

Using the Selection field, left click on the arrow and choose how you want to search for the payments you want to look at:

Customer:  Search for a particular customerpayment

Supplier:  Search for payments made to a supplier

Method:  Search by the method of payment (ie: cash, Amex)

Date Range: Search for payments within a certain date range

GL Code  Search for payments made to a certain Gl Code

 

Left click on the above option you want to search by.

 

Searching By Customer

 

If you choose Customer, a Customers window appears.

 

Left click anywhere on the customer list, and type a word or part of a word which relates to the customer code. This will highlight the record you are looking for, or the closest match.

 

Hit the Enter key, or left click on the Select button, or double left click on the customer.

 

This will bring up the Payment Register window, with that customers payments.

 

Left click the Clear button to start over

 

Left click the Print button to print the window and the Progress window will appear to print the payment details. This will show you a copy of the Payment Register Report.To enlarge or decrease this on screen, left click on the magnifying glass symbol on the top left hand corner, and choose the size you wish to view the report at.

 

Left click on the print symbol to print, or the print symbol with the cross over it not to print (both these symbols are located in the top left-hand corner). Both of these options will return you to the Payment Register window.

 

To choose another customer, left click on the button to the right of the Customer field. (note: this field name changes for each different Selection you wish to search by. Hence, this step is the same for all different selections).

Searching by Supplier

 

If you choose to search by Supplier, a Supplier window will pop up.

 

To search, view and print supplier payments you carry out the same steps as you would to view customer payments.

 

Searching by Method

 

When you choose to search by Method, a Method File window appears.

Left click on the list and type in a word or part of word which relates to the Method of payment. Or simply left click on the method of payment, if you can see it.

 

Once the method you want is highlighted, either double click on it, hit the Enter key, or left click the Select button.

 

The Payment Register window will then appear, displaying any payments (made or received) where that method was used (in the example below, a cheque).

 

Printing, Clearing or choosing a different method are the same steps as for previous Selections.

 

Searching by Date Range

 

When you choose to search by Date Range, simply left click in the Date Range for Selection From and To fields, and type in the date range you wish to search under.

 

Hit the Enter key, or left click on the Refresh button.

 

This will bring up all the invoices for payments within that date range.

 

Printing and clearing follow the same steps as the previous selections. To change the Date Range simply perform step 24 again.

 

When you have finished viewing the Payment Register, left click the Close button, or click on the X on the top right hand corner.

 

Searching by Gl Code

 

When you choose to search by GL Code, simply left click in the GL Code for Selection the Gl Selection window will appear. Select the ledger code you wish to view by pressing the Enter key, or left double click on the selected account.

 

This will bring up all the payments that were allocated to the selected Ledger Code.

 

For Printing and clearing follow the same steps as the previous selections. You may view another ledger by clicking on the Selection Button to the right of the GL Code and selecting a new ledger code.

 

When you have finished viewing the Payment Register, left click the Close button, or click on the X on the top right hand corner.
