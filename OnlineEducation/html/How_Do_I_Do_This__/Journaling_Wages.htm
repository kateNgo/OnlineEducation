Journaling Wages

 

Some people who use payroll software such as Logisoft payroll choose to journal their wages rather than using the wage entry system. In this case you should journal the wages every week as follows.

 

Credit the bank account 1020 (or other account) by the net wages

Debit the employee wage account (s) by the net wage amounts

Credit the 2040 payg tax account by the total of the payg.

Debit the employee payg expense account(s) by the payg amounts.

Credit the Super holding account 2041 for the super amount

Debit the employee super expense accounts for the super.

 

You may process tax and wages into the same account meaning that the gross figure will be in the account. You may also process the wages all to the same expense account or to separate accounts for each employee. Sound confusing? My suggestion is use the wage entry system 
