Ledger Departments

 

Did you know that you can setup as many as 9 different cost centres or departments in the general ledger masterfiles? You can then get a profit and loss report on each of the departments as if they were separate businesses of sections of the business.

 

If your business has more than one profit centre then you can setup department 1 as the income and expense department for one area and department 2 as the income and expense for another area and then department 3 and so on. Department 0 means that the expense or revenue items are common to all departments and will be attributed to the bottom line income or expenses for the entire business as a whole.
