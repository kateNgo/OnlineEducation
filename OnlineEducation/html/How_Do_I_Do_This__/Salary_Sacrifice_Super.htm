Salary Sacrifice Super

 

HOW TO ENTER SALARY SACRIFICE SUPER

 

This method is where the employee gains a tax benefit from Personal Super Contribution.

 

Firstly, you need to reduce the Gross by the value of the Employee Contribution, thus reducing the tax and net wage. Then you manually increase the value of the super on the wages screen.

 

You must create a ledger called Salary Sacrifice in the same number series as the Wages Ledger. Then do a Journal from the Super Expense Account to the Salary Sacrifice Expense Account.

 

HOW TO ENTER MEMBER VOLUNTARY SUPER

 

This method is where the employeetax is not affected.

 

Firstly, do the Wage Entry as normal. Account for the Member Voluntary as a non taxable deduction, which will reduce the Net Wage, but leave the Tax at the normal rate. Increase the super on the wages screen manually.

 

Then a Journal needs to be done to decrease the Super Expense Account and increase the Wages Expense Account.
