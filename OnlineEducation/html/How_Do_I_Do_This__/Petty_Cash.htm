Petty Cash

 

There are various ways to handle and process Petty Cash in AM-Win. The way we usually recommended is to setup a bank account (say 1022) called Petty Cash through the Ledger Masterfiles. Start with a petty cash float of lets say $200 which you get from either journaling the money from the bank account or from another cash account. You can then make payments for goods such as tea, coffee, milk, lunches and other charges through the cheque entry or supplier payment area out of 1022 petty cash (bank) account. You can then do a Journal Entry for reimbursing the petty cash whereby you credit your bank account and debit the Petty Cash (1022) account.

 

Reconciling the petty cash

 

Because the petty cash is setup as a bank account you can now use the Bankrec Module to reconcile the petty cash entries.
