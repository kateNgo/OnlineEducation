Check Tran Masteriles

 

This option is a data validity check for your Customers, suppliers and also your ledger items. The system checks the balance of your customer, supplier and ledger accounts compared to the opening balance for the financial year plus all transactions for the current year. If the carried forward balance plus the transactions do not match the balance of the customer, ledger or supplier then a list of out of balance accounts will be displayed for you.

 

Customers

 

Click on the customer radio button and then click on the Process Button. This may take a while to process all of the records and across a network could take several minutes.

 

Once completed if you get a message saying No Records found then this means all of your customer balances reconcile to the transactions. This is what you want to see. Alternatively a list of out of balance customers will be displayed. This list shows the Customer Code, Name, Balance Fwd, Balance Fwd + Transactions, Balance Fwd + Current year balances, Transaction balance, Masterfile balance and the Difference amount. The difference amount is what the customer is out of balance by. You must then print an enquiry for the customer from the Enquiries option and research why the customer is not balanced. Once you know this you can then fix the error by an invoice, credit etc.

Ledger

 

Click on the Ledger radio button and then click on the Process Button. This may take a while to process all of the records and across a network could take several minutes.

 

Once completed if you get a message saying No Records found then this means all of your customer balances reconcile to the transactions. You will also get a message saying If your trial balance does not balance then click the Fix Button now. Alternatively a list of out of balance customers will be displayed. This list shows the Ledger Code, Name, Balance Fwd, Balance Fwd + Transactions, Balance Fwd + Current year balances, Transaction balance, Masterfile balance and the Difference amount. The difference amount is what the ledger is out of balance by.

 

If out of balance entries exist or your trial balance does not balance then click on the FIX button. The system will then adjust the ledger balance to agree with the transaction balance and post any discrepancy in the trial balance to the 3020 ledger account. You can then use Journal Entries to move this amount to an appropriate account of your choice.

 

NOTE: Prior to pressing the FIX button you may wish to print the Transaction Audit report in an attempt to find out why the transactions are out of balance.

 

Print

You may also choose to print a list of the out of balance accounts for your reference by clicking on the Print Button once the customer, supplier or ledger option has been selected.
