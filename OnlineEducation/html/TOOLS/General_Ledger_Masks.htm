GENERAL LEDGER MASKS

 

This option can be used to choose which general ledger accounts to display for specific transactions. This means that when you are invoicing labour, only labour accounts are displayed and when you are using expenses only the expense accounts are displayed.

 

 

Go to the Tools menu and choose General Ledger Masks. The General Ledger Masks window will appear.

For each account left click the buttons on the right of the From and To fields. This will bring up the General Ledger window.

 

Using the locator button, find the GL code you want, and left click the Select button. This will insert this code into the relevant field in the General Ledger Mask window.

 

In the above example, the General Ledger accounts which will be displayed will be codes from 1010  1020 and 1900-1999

 

Left click the Save button before moving to GL account in the Name column.

 

To choose the masks for another GL account highlight the account in the Name column, and then follow the above steps, remembering to click the Save button each time you are about to move to another account.

 

Left click the Close button to close the General Ledger Mask window.
