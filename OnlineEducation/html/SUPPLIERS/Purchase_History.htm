PURCHASE HISTORY

 

Purchase History is used to look up details of purchases of Parts from Suppliers.

 

Go to the Suppliers menu and choose Purchase History. The SupplierHistory window will appear.

 

 Note: if you cannot see all the following columns: Date, Ref #,Order #, Age, Supplier, GST, Total, Paid_Amt, Balance_Amt and Type then place your mouse cursor on the lines which separate each column. You should then be able to make the columns bigger or smaller, by moving these lines, in order to fit all the columns in.

 

The information is in order from the most recent to the least recent purchase.

 

If you left click on the Supplier tab, you may search by Supplier.

 

If you want to search outside the one year date range which will automatically be inserted in the From and To fields, left click in those fields and type in the preferred date range.

 

Left click on the button next to the Supplier Code field. A window with a list of Suppliers will come up. Select the Supplier from this window.

 

The details of purchases from that specific supplier will be shown in the Purchase History window (as shown above).

 

To search by Part number, left click on the Part tab. A new window will appear. You can change the date range to search by as in Step 4.

 

Left click on the button to the right of the Part Num field. The Select Stock or Alternate Stock window will appear.

 

Once the part has been selected, the details of purchases of that part from all Suppliers during the chosen date range will be displayed.

 

You now have the option for printing either a detailed or a summary report while in Suppliers History.

 

Suppliers History is found under the Suppliers menu. Choose purchase history. The Suppliers History window will appear.

 

The Print Detail and Print Summary buttons are located at the top right hand corner of the screen.

 

By pressing the Print Summary button while you are in the date tab, you will produce a report listing all records.

 

By highlighting a record in the date tab and clicking on the Print Detail you will produce a report only for that particular record.

 

Within the Supplier or Part tab (accessed by clicking on the tabat the top of the Suppliers History window), clicking the Print Detail button will produce a report

 

showing details of each item within a purchase order. Clicking the Print Summary will produce a report which contains the total amount of the purchase order.

 

Credit request reprint in purchase history

 

You are able to reprint a credit request from the supplier history option under the Suppliers menu from within AM-Win. When you have selected the supplier and highlight a credit note, the Credit Rpt button at the top of the screen becomes available and you can then reprint the credit request.

 

Left click the Close button when you have finished using Purchase History.
