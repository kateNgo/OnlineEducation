The Automatic Price Adjustment Window

 

When stock is selected, the Cost field is automatically filled in. If you change the amount in the Cost field, the Automatic Price Adjustment window will appear.

This window shows the % you have changed the old price by. It also displays the old prices, as well as automatically changing these prices to keep the same profit margin you had before.

 

To keep the old prices, left click the Ignore button.

 

To change the prices to the figures in the New Price fields, left click the Update button.
