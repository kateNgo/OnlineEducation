Reverse Payment - Supplier

 

Reversing Payments

 

A new Reverse Payments section has been added where you can reverse payments without going into Open Item/Batch payments and doing a negative payments and without having to issue a credit note when either of the following situations occur:

When a Suppliers cheque is dishonoured; and when the payment you received has been credited in the wrong Supplier account

 

For Suppliers, go to the Supplier Menu > Payments> Reverse Payments

 

In the new window which appears click on the button next to code and select the Supplier. The window will then display payments which have been made by that Supplier. All these payments will have green ticks next to them.

 

From the payment register, click onto the payment you want to reverse. The details of this payment will then be shown in the Cheque, Description Fields, Method field etc. You are able to change the date, Cheque Ref Number and Description.

 

Click on the process button to process the Reverse Payment.

 

Next time you look up this screen for this Supplier, the payment you reversed will appear with a red R next to it.
