Batch Payments

 

Supplier batch payments allows you to pay several payments to several Suppliers in one batch entry process.

 

Select the Batch entry option under the Supplier > Payments menu.

 

First you must locate the Supplier you wish to pay by typing in the first few letters of the Suppliers code. Once the Supplier is found you can then press the insert key or click on the insert button. The date will then automatically be defaulted to todaydate. You can change the date manually or select it from the calendar button.

 

Supplier Payment Method  Click on the arrow to the right of the method field and select the method of payment between accounts 1010  1029.

 

Select the age of the payment amount owing that is being paid. If the amount owing covers more than one age then select the oldest age.

 

NOTE: batch payments will automatically pay from the oldest to the newest invoice in the age you have selected. If you do not wish to pay certain invoices then you must use the open item payments option.

 

Description  Enter description, eg. Account Payment, July account etc

 

Cheque/Ref  Enter cheque reference or details, if any. If the payment was a direct deposit then you may wish to enter the bank statement page or some other identifying reference.

 

Amount  Enter the amount received by the Supplier or to be allocated to the payment and then press enter or click on the update button. This will move the record into the bottom of the screen.

 

Select the next Supplier to pay and then click the insert button to record the next payment.

 

You can change a existing record by clicking on it at the bottom of the screen and then changing the details. You must then click the update button to save the changes.

 

When all records are entered you can then click on the Process button or press the F12 key to process the records.

 

Details Button.  The details button at the bottom of the screen shows all invoices for the selected Supplier including paid, part paid and unpaid invoices.
