Purchase Credits

 

PURCHASE CREDITS

 

Purchase credits is an alternative to Purchase Orders. The difference lies in the fact that purchase credits allow purchases to be batched in for a particular supplier for multiple invoices to the suppliers. However, purchase orders only allow one purchase to be done at a time. Purchase credits is also a quicker option if orders are not required to be printed.

 

Go to the Suppliers menu and choose Purchase_Credits. The Purchase/Credits screen will open.

 

For Purchases

 

Make sure there is a dot in the circle next to Purchase at the top of the window.

 

Left click the button next to the Code field. A window with a list of suppliers will appear. Select the Supplier you want. You will then return back to the main window.

 

Left click the Insert button.

 

Date: this will default to todaydate. However, it should be the date that appears on the invoice. Left click into this field and type in the date if it is to be changed.

 

Ref/Invoice: type in the reference or invoice number into this field

 

Stk/GL code: left click on the arrow next to this field. Choose from the small menu, either Stock Code or GL Code. Choose Stock if the item bought is a stock item Choose GL Code if: the item/s bought are anything other than stock, or are non Stock items.

 

IF YOU CHOOSE GL CODE:

 

The next field will have GL Code written on top of it.

 

Left click on the button next to this field. This will bring up a new window where you can select the GL Code. Once you have selected it, it will be displayed in the Purchase Credits main window. The range of codes that can be selected is decided by your settings for expenses in the general ledger masks section.

Left click on the arrows next to the GST Types field, & choose 1,2,3 or 4.

1 = GST 2 = NO GST 3 = ACQUISITION 4 = M/VEHICLE

 

Type in the Quantity in the Qty field (ie- amount of items purchased).

 

Type in the Cost in the Cost field. This is the amount you pay for each item, BEFORE GST.

 

Once you click out of these fields, the Amount field will be automatically filled in. This field can be changed if you do want the Amount to be different.

 

The Description field will be filled in when the GL code is chosen. This description can be changed to indicate what was purchased, by typing into the Description field.

 

Left click the Update button.

 

The information will now be added to the bottom of the window, and the fields will be cleared.

 

Left click on the Insert button to do another purchase, either for Stock or for a GL code.

 

Once you have completed adding the information left click the Process button. This will process the information and clear the screen.

 

IF YOU CHOOSE STOCK CODE: the next field will have Stock written on top of it, and a Job # and another Qty field will be added. These new fields only need to be used when the To Job button is used :

 

Left click on the button next to the Stock field. This will bring up the Select stock or Alternate Stock window. When the stock is selected its code will be displayed in the above Stock field.

 

Type in the Quantity delivered in the Qty field.

The Cost field will be automatically filled in when the stock is selected, however this field may be changed. The Amount field will be automatically filled in. This field can be changed if you do want the Amount to be different. If you manually change the cost amounts then a price change window will appear. This will allow you to change your selling prices according to changes in your buying price.

 

The Amount field will be automatically filled in.

 

Left click the Update button and the information will be inserted into the bottom of the window, and the fields will be cleared.

 

To add in more entries, left click the Insert button and repeat the above process.

 

Left click the Process button to process the purchase and to clear the screen.

 

The To Job button: If the stock purchased is to be used/is being used for a particular job, left click the To Job-F2 button. You can only press this if steps 18-21 have been completed.

 

This will bring up a Browse Jobs in Progress window.

 

Select the job that the item is being used for, by highlighting it and clicking on the Select button, or pressing the Enter key. This will return you to the Purchase credits main window, where the job number of the job you have selected will be displayed In the Job No. field.

 

Qty: type in the quantity of items to be used for the job you have selected in step 27. Note: this must be LESS than the quantity which appears in the main quantity field (situated next to the Cost field).
