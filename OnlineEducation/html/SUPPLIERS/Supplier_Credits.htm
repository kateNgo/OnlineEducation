Supplier Credits

 

Credit Notes

 

Left click on Cr_Note at the top of the Purchase/Credits window. This will mark a black dot into the circle for Credit Notes. The following screen will appear, which is slightly different to the Purchases window.

 

Select the Supplier as for purchases by pressing the button next to the Code field.

 

To Insert an invoice, left click the Invoice button. This will bring up a list of previous purchases from this supplier. Double click or select the invoice which brings the invoice into the credit screen. You cannot credit a higher qty or amount than was on the original invoice.

 

Follow steps for doing Purchases

 

You must also select one of the From Acc fields, by left clicking on them so that there is a black dot in the circle for the Account you have chosen.

 

Account: money will come out of amount owing to supplier

Bank 1,2,3: the different bank accounts the money will come out of

Cash: paying by cash.

 

If you wish to credit a whole invoice, left click the Invoice button, instead of the Insert button.

 

The Browse the Purchase Register window appears displaying the invoices you owe to the supplier.

 

Highlight the Transaction number you want, and left click the Select button.

 

This will enter this transaction in the Purchase/Credits window.

 

Note: when you use this invoice button you cannot credit more than the amount which is showing on the invoice.

 

Either left click the Process button, OR highlight the transaction, left click the Update button, and then left click the Process button.

 

When the Process button is pressed when doing a Credit Note, a new window will appear.

 Note:  P Amount = payable amount

 B Amount = balance amount

 

Back button: If you left click the Back button, you will be returned to the previous screen in the Purchase/Credits window.

 

Toggle button: The toggle button selects the invoice you want to take credit away on. When an Invoice number is highlighted and the Toggle button is clicked on, the Left 2 Post amount will subtract the amount in the B Amount column for that invoice. A symbol will also appear in the Tag column.

In the example above: the fourth invoice has been tagged.

If the Toggle button is clicked on again while this invoice is highlighted, it will untag it, and the Left 2 Post will return to itoriginal amount.

 

If the Autopost button is left clicked on, this will keep tagging the invoices until the Left 2 Post fields shows a zero amount.

If you highlight an invoice number, and if there is an amount in the P Amount column, the Change button allows you to change the P Amount.

 

Hence, if highlighted the invoice number with P amount = 50, the Altering Amount window will appear with this value.

 

Type in the amount you want to change the P Amount to and then click the OK button. Note: the B amount will remain the same.

 

If you press cancel, the P Amount will return to $0.00.

 

Once the P Amount has changed, the Left 2 Post field will consequently re-adjust.

 

Note: if you have already changed an amount, and wish to change it again, when you click the change button, the amount which appears in the Altering Amount window will be the ORIGINAL amount that was in the P Amount column.

 

When the Left to Post field equals ZERO, click the Final button. This will finalise the credit note and clear the screen.
