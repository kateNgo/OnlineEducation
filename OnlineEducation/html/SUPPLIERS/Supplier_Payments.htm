Supplier Payments

 

SUPPLIER PAYMENT

 

This option is utilised for when you wish to make a payment to a supplier for an outstanding account/s.

 

To make a payment:

 

In the AM-WIN window, click the SupplierPayment icon (the 9th icon from the left) or from the menu choose Suppliers, left click and choose Payments, left click.

 

The Supplier Payment (Open item) window appears.

 

Code  Left click the button in the right of the Code field to enter the Suppliercode. The Suppliers window appears.

 

Enter Supplier Code or select the supplier by left clicking on the supplier. This highlights the supplier to whom the payment is being made. Choose Select.

 

The details of the supplier and the amounts owing on the any invoices outstanding are automatically inserted into the Supplier Payment window.

 

Note : if the supplier has not been allocated with an ABN (Australian Business Number, then you must take % of the payment to the supplier).

 

Refer to the Customer Payment chapter and follow the steps outlined to make payment to a supplier.

 

To process the supplier payment, choose Process. The ledger accounts will be automatically updated

 

When processing a Customer or Supplier payment you are now able to click on the date field heading from within the payment window and sort the outstanding invoices into date order. This may make it easier to process outstanding invoices for a particular date range. From within the customer and also the supplier payments module you are able to produce a report detailing all of the outstanding invoices for the selected customer or supplier. This can then be used to reconcile the accounts.

 

When a customer receipt is printed the transaction number for that receipt is also printed on the receipt to represent a receipt number. Because the transaction number is used which references back to the payment, the next receipt printed may not be the next transaction number as other transactions such as invoices or journal entries may have been processed between payments being processed.

 

To view details for a particular purchase order, left click on the invoice and this highlights the invoice to be viewed, select Details. The Purchase History window appears.

 

To clear the Supplier Payment window, select Clear.
