Consignment Stock

 

CONSIGNMENT STOCK

 

This option was specifically designed for the Marine Industry and caters for consignment stock such as outboard motors and boats. It may also be used for the automotive industry in respect to Consignment Stock but has not been designed for large quantities of smaller items.

 

Consignment Stock is found under the Consignment menu (which is found between the Enquiry and Others menu).

 

In the Consignment menu choose Consignment Stock.

 

Click on the Insert button, or click the Insert key on the keyboard.

 

Fill in the Make, Model and Serial fields with the appropriate details.

 

Click on the arrow next to the Status field. A menu will pop down with the options of Onhand, Sold or Returned.

 

Choose OnHand for Consignment Stock which you have in stock.

 

Choose Returned for Consignment Stock which you have returned to the supplier

 

You cannot choose Sold. Once this stock has been sold and an invoice has been done, this field will display Sold when you enter in the Consignment Stock window.

 

Trust Receipt: Type in the Invoice/Receipt number

 

Acquired: use the up and down arrows next to this field to find the date when this Consignment Stock was purchased

 

Interest Starts: use the up and down arrows next to this field to find the date when interest started to accumulate on this item.

 

Fill in the Cost Ex GST, Cost Inc GST, Retail, Trade, Wholesale, N/Wholesale fields with the appropriate prices. Please note: this retail field (on the left hand side of the window) is the retail price that the item is sold at to the customer.

 

Date: click on the button next to the Date field to use the Calender to choose the date the item was sold.

 

The field below the Date field: This field will be automatically filled in with the Customer Name of who the item was sold to, once the item has been sold and an invoice produced.

 

If you chose Returned as a status, this field under the date field will become a Return No. field. You would then type in the Returned Receipt no. into this field.

 

Amount: this field cannot be filled in when the status is OnHand or Returned. When the status is Sold, type into this field the amount the consignment stock was sold for.

 

At Rate: use the arrows next to this field to choose the rate that the Interest is being calculated on for this item.

 

Extra Cost: Fill in this field with the amount of any additional cost for this item, such as interest or maintenance

 

Units: this would be the units for this item, such as ÂeachÂ or ÂsetÂ etc

 

Retail: In this retail field on the right hand side of the window, type in the retail price the Supplier sold the Stock to you for.

 

Sales Ledger: Click on the button next to this field. A Select Revenue Ledger window will appear.

 

Choose the ledger account which you want the money to go into once this stock item is sold, by highlighting it and then clicking the Select button. Please note: this ledger account can only be between 6020 and 6029.

 

Once all these fields are filled in, click the OK button.

 

The item will then be added into the left hand Serial column.

 

You can use the Locator button to sort through the items using the Serial number.

 

If you click the other tabs such as : by Model, by Make, by Trust Receipt the items will be ordered by whatever tab you pressed- however, the locator will not work in these tabs- only in the Serial tab.

 

To Change a Record: highlight it in the Serial column and then click on the Change button.

 

Change any details you wish, and then click the OK button.

 

To Delete a Record: highlight the record in the Serial column and then click on the Delete button.

 

CONSIGNMENT STOCK PRELOAD OPTIONS

This option allows you to load the Make, Model and Trust Receipt number from a consignment stock item, and apply these details to future consignment stock items, without having to retype these details each time. This is done by selecting the fast forward button in the small screen on the top right corner of the main screen.

 

In the Serial column, highlight the item whose details you want to load.

 

The details will then appear in the Consignment Stock Preload Options window:

 

When you click the Insert button in the main Consignment Stock window, these details can be inserted into the new record by clicking the rewind button, saving you from typing them again.

 

In order to Clear these details, so you can enter different details, click on the Clear button in the Consignment Stock Preload Options window which is shown with an eraser icon.
