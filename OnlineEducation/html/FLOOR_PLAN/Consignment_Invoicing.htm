Consignment Stock in Job Invoicing

 

There is a Consignment Stock button within Job Invoicing which allows you to insert a Consignment Stock item into an invoice.

 

Within the Job Invoicing screen, the Consignment Stock button is located in the bottom left hand side of the screen.

 

It can also be accessed by pressing by Alt and C together on the keyboard.

 

When the button is pressed the Consignment Stock window appears.

 

Select the item you want to add to the invoice, by highlighting the item and then clicking the Select button.

 

The Insert Consign Record Window will then appear.

 

Click the OK button.

 

The item will then be added into the invoice.
